Seminars and Workshops
Seminars and workshops are offered to provide professionals the opportunity to stay abreast of cutting-edge technologies, current applications and techniques. Continuing Education Units (CEUs), professional development hours (PDHs) and International Learning Units (ILUs) are awarded to attendees. Formats range from two-hour seminars to longer, more intensive workshops. Registration is available online and certificates and certifications are awarded upon completion of courses.
Customized On Site Training
As part of its services, the PE provides customized training programs for organizations. This enables professionals to take advantage of practical applications within the organization to apply the theory involved with the training. It is possible to arrange for PE courses to be taught on site at a company’s facility. To initiate and discuss the options for customized training, contact the Professional Education Department’s director at
gpe@msoe.edu.

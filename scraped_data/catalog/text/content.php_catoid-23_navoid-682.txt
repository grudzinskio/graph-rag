Professional Education
MSOE provides the latest in technical knowledge and leadership training for professional in industries ranging from manufacturing to health care to education. Whether you’re early in your career or at the top of the organizational chart, MSOE can help push you to the next level with a variety of practical professional development courses. All training courses award students with Continuing Education Units (CEUs) or Professional Development Hours (PDHs).
MSOE is highly ranked and has been known for delivering rigorous and relevant education since 1903. Many of our Professional Education students come to us via word of mouth because managers know that MSOE’s training is a smart investment. It’s not uncommon for a company to send one person to an MSOE course and then bring back a group because they were so impressed with the content.
Our industry partners frequently turn to MSOE’s experts to solve real-world problems. In the Professional Education department alone, we’ve worked with more than 200 companies over the past five years, and so we’re plugged into the current challenges - and successful strategies - of companies just like yours.
We hire the best of the best, and our instructors aren’t locked away in the ivory tower - these are industry experts who often have decades of experience in the field.
Our Professional Education classes typically range from 8-10 students but can be even smaller. That means you’ll really get to know your instructor and classmates in an intimate workshop setting and will have the time to delve into your personal workplace challenges.
Customize your training.
Don’t see the perfect fit in our course catalog? No problem. We’re a one-stop resource for specialized custom training and consulting. We’ll visit your workplace to assess the needs and create a custom training program just for you and your colleagues.

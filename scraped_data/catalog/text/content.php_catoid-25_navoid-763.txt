Professional Education
MSOE provides the best in applied technical knowledge and leadership training for professionals in industries ranging from manufacturing to health care. Whether you’re early in your career or at the top of the organizational chart, MSOE can take your thinking to the next level with a variety of practical professional development courses. All training seminars and courses award students with Continuing Education Units (CEUs) or Professional Development Hours (PDHs).
MSOE is highly ranked and known for delivering rigorous and relevant hands-on applied education since 1903. The wider business community considers MSOE a smart investment and our faculty are industry experts with the ability to solve-real world problems. In the Professional Education department alone, we’ve worked with more than 200 companies over the past five years, so we’re plugged into the current challenges - and successful strategies - of companies just like yours.
Our courses and seminars are offered online and in person. Offerings are interactive, and you will enjoy learning, asking questions, and sharing your experiences with other participants. Flexible course schedules can range from full or half days to 2-hour increments over several weeks. That means you’ll really get to know your instructor and classmates in an intimate workshop setting and will have the time to delve into your personal workplace challenges.
Customize your training.
Don’t see the perfect fit in our course catalog? No problem. We’re your resource for specialized custom training and consulting. We’ll visit your workplace in person or online to assess your needs and create a custom training program just for you.

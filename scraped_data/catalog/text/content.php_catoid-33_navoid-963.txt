Professional Education
Taking classes through MSOE’s Rader School of Business is a great way to learn new skills, expand your knowledge base, broaden your expertise, and enhance your resume. Professional development of this kind can give a competitive edge to individuals who are applying for jobs, switching industries, or moving up the career ladder. Course takers earn “professional credits” or “certificates of completion” for each class to demonstrate their accomplishments.
Customized Learning Options
Whether in-person, online or as a hybrid course, MSOE’s customized learning options make it possible for you or your employees to directly benefit from our expertise.  Our experts can work directly with you and your personnel to quickly develop specialized training to meet your current operational objectives.  Whether it is helping your company deal with compound business risks, design of experiments, difficult stakeholders, troubled projects, reading blueprints and instrumentation, or other complex challenges, MSOE’s experts can put the right training together for you.
Discover how MSOE can help your business. Email us at
gpe@msoe.edu
or call us at (800) 321-6763 to get started today.

MG 5904 - Advanced Project Management: Financial Concepts and Techniques
0
lecture hours
0
lab hours
3
credits
Individuals who successfully complete the Project Management Lead Certificate offered through the Business Excellence Consortium receive credit for this course and EM-5904. The BEC offers a seven-day Project Management Lead Certificate, which is the second of three certificate levels. This program prepares you and your team to better organize, implement and control projects. All phases of project management are addressed. You will immediately apply what you have learned to your present job. The program uses and integrated approach so you learn key aspects of project management each day. Instructors are part of MSOE’s Rader School of Business faculty who possess extensive project management experience. This program provides participants with the tools and knowledge necessary to manage a wide range of projects, regardless of their environment.

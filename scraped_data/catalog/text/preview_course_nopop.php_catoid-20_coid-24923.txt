AE 2250 - Specifications and Contracts
2
lecture hours
0
lab hours
2
credits
Course Description
This course provides a working knowledge of the principles for writing effective specifications for building systems and materials, as well as interpreting standard construction industry contracts.  Students will learn the formatting and writing requirements for specifications used in the construction industry, utilizing the CSI MasterFormat.  Students will also review standard contracts such as AIA and EJCDC common form contracts.   (prereq:
AE 1001
or
CV 1001
)
Course Learning Outcomes
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
Describe the nature and responsibilities undertaken by parties to a contract
Understand the basic terms and conditions in AIA and EJCDC contracts
Describe typical construction project delivery methods
Organize, prepare, interpret, and write construction specifications
Understand the CSI MasterFormat system of organizing specifications

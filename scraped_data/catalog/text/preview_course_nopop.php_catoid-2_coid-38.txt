AE 3311 - Introduction to Building Information Modeling II
1
lecture hours
2
lab hours
1
credits
This course prepares the student to utilize building information modeling (BIM) as a coordinated, integrated and consistent approach to a building project in design and construction decision making. Students are provided the basics to produce high-quality 3-D designs and construction documents, along with cost-estimating, and construction planning. The students will use BIM in the Senior Project sequence. The course will utilize Autodesk Revit Building Systems.   (prereq: junior standing,
AE 1301
,
AE 1312
)

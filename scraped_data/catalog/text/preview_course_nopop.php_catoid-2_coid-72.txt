AF 2964 - Air Force Field Training
6
lecture hours
0
lab hours
6
credits
Off-campus summer program held at Maxwell Air Force Base, Montgomery, Ala. The program provides the student with practical leadership experience and extensive practical training in fundamental leadership and military skills. Students do not incur military obligation, do not pay expenses, but do receive pay for this training. This course is offered in lieu of
AF 1011
,
AF 1012
,
AF 1013
,
AF 2021
,
AF 2022
, and/or
AF 2023
at the direction of the department chair for students pursuing an Air Force Commission. S/U grade assessment.  Offered only during the summer. (prereq: consent of department chair)

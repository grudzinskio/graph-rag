Course Description
(MU: AFAS 1011) AIR 1011 is designed to introduce students to the United States Air Force and provide an overview of the basic characteristics, missions and organization of the Air Force. Includes an introduction to communication skills.   (prereq: none) (coreq: students pursuing an Air Force commission must also be concurrently enrolled in

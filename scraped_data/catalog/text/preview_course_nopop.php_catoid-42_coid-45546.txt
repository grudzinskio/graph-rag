RSC 4011 - Fourth-Year Seminar II
1
lecture hours
0
lab hours
1
credits
Course Description
This spring seminar is designed to support the retention and success of fourth-year MSOE students and students of senior standing through culturally centered experiences and activities that foster community engagement at MSOE. The instructional framework is based on the
Council for the Advancement of Standards of Higher Education’s Learning and Development Domains and Outcomes (2020)
. It builds on Fourth-Year Seminar I, with an emphasis on interpersonal competence: leadership.
Prereq:
Coordinator consent
Note:
This seminar is offered only in spring.
This course meets the following Raider Core CLO Requirement:
None
Course Learning Outcomes
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
Participate in one or more service/volunteer activities that are characterized by reciprocity
Demonstrate skill in guiding and assisting a group, organization, or community in meeting its goals
Exhibit democratic principles as a leader or group member
Prerequisites by Topic
None
Coordinator
Seandra Mitchell, Katie Toske

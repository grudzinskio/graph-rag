NUR 2035 - Certified Nursing Assistant Transfer Credit
0
lecture hours
0
lab hours
1
credits
Course Description
This course provides one credit of recognition for prior learning/practice in the School of Nursing for certified nursing assistants. It is not intended as a regular course offering at MSOE.
Prereq:
None
Note:
Students must provide proof of active Certified Nursing Assistant certification and have completed the transfer credit process.
This course meets the following Raider Core CLO Requirement:
None
Course Learning Outcomes
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
This course recognizes the knowledge and skills obtained through a CNA course and examination.
Prerequisites by Topic
Current CNA certification validated by the MSOE transfer credit process.
Course Topics
General skills validated in CNA examinations include:
Infection control principles and PPE
Personal hygiene, bedmaking and toileting
Nutrition and feeding
Safe patient handling- bed positioning, ambulation and transfers with a gait belt
Catheter care and elimination needs
Coordinator
Dr. Amy Ketchum

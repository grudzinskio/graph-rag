The STEM Education certificate is designed for college or K-12 teachers who have a minimum of three years of STEM teaching experience. The certificate builds on and enhances existing STEM teaching skills and knowledge by providing a framework within which teachers can develop, implement, and share evidence-based delivery practices focused on improving student engagement and achievement in the STEM disciplines.
Required Courses:
The STEM education certificate requires completion of:
MB 6001 - STEM Mindset and Methods

MB 6100 - Statistical Thinking and Data Analytics

MB 7210 - Evaluating Educational Interventions

Understanding the relationship between technology and business practices is key in maintaining a competitive edge in business. Knowledge in both business processes and technology components and systems is necessary to successfully implement business information systems. Key skills involve the ability to analyze business processes, select appropriate information systems, perform feasibility analysis, requirements analysis, and develop implementation strategies.

The Minor in Physics is offered to those students who wish to expand their background and understanding of physics. To qualify, a student must take 28 credits of eligible courses. This includes a minimum of 16 credits from the list of approved courses.
Interested students should contact the coordinator for the minor for clarification to the extent to which free, technical and math/science electives may be used in fulfilling the requirements for the minor.
For additional information, see the
minors policy
.

PH 499

PH 199

The technical communication minor is designed to enhance students’ professional communication skills and prepare them for personal and workplace communication challenges. Courses in the minor teach students how to design and manage print and digital professional documents, develop and deliver high-impact professional presentations, work effectively in a team environment, and develop competencies in resolving conflicts at multiple levels. These skills can be transferred to careers in a wide range of fields, including today’s fast-growing science and healthcare communication, engineering and technology fields. The minor complements many MSOE majors, particularly those in engineering, nursing, business, and actuarial science programs. The TC minor requires a minimum of 25 quarter credits in TC and approved HU or SS courses.

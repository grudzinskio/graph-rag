The Machine Learning Graduate Certificate is a set of application-oriented courses fusing concepts from statistics and computer science to design algorithms that process data, make predictions, and help make decisions. Upon completion, the student will be able to synthesize solutions in the artificial intelligence and machine learning domains and be able to connect the knowledge they have gained to their professional work.

An asterisk indicates if the course covers more than one CLO. If a course covers more than one CLO, students must choose which CLO they will apply to the Raider Core.
Courses may not be double-counted toward more than one Raider Core requirement.
Raider Core Courses Covering Exhibit Curiosity
Periodically, courses may be offered on special topics that may also cover a CLO. These courses are numbered 2980, 3980, or 4980, with the prefix from the discipline offering the course. The CLO assigned to the course will be noted in Scheduler.

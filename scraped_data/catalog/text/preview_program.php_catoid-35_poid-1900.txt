The courses below cover at least one of
Collaborate Successfully, Embrace Diversity, Exhibit Curiosity
,
Think Critically,
and
Demonstrate Ethical Understanding
CLOs and are eligible toward the Raider Core elective. Click on the course description to see which CLO(s) the course covers.
Courses may not be double-counted toward more than one Raider Core requirement.
Students should check their program curriculum page for any limitations on the choice of course for the Raider Core elective.
Consult Scheduler to determine what courses are offered in a given term.
Raider Core Elective Courses
Periodically, courses may be offered on special topics that also cover a CLO. These courses are numbered 2980, 3980, or 4980, with the prefix from the discipline offering the course. The CLO assigned to the course will be indicated in Scheduler.

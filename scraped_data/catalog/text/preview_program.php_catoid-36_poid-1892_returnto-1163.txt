Overview
This certificate will serve as a follow-on to the Graduate Certificate in Applied Machine Learning. It will provide deep technical content that will enable the student to bring machine learning solutions into production in the student’s industry. It can be completed in nine months of part-time study. For additional information, see the
Certificates
policy.

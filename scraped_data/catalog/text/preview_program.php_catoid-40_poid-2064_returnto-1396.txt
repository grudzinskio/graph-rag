Overview
Marketing is about fulfilling customer needs and wants. At its core, Marketing focuses on Managing Relationships Profitably. The Marketing degree explores all aspects related to marketing relationships: Business-to-Consumer, Business-to-Business, and Consumer-to-Consumer through knowledge of marketing research, brand management, advertising, consumer behavior, and more.
Program Outcomes
Graduates with a B.S. in Marketing from the Rader School of Business will be able to:
Strategically analyze, evaluate, and solve business problems using technology and an understanding of the social context
Prepare and deliver, concise, persuasive stakeholder-centered information in a professional manner
Develop leaders of character who promote effectiveness in working with and leading diverse teams
Exhibit an entrepreneurial mindset
Develop and design advertising campaign across media

Marketing, B.S. Version S1B (German Study Abroad Program, MSOE students)
This map is taken by students in the Marketing major who enroll at Technische Hochschule Lübeck (THL) in Lübeck, Germany, for their third year. Students follow the regular Marketing major path for their first, second, and fourth years.
Year Three (MSOE students at THL)
There are over 20 courses delivered in English to choose from at THL.
Students need to earn 30 semester credits (10 courses) to stay on track while at THL.
Students need to meet with their academic advisor before travel to ensure the appropriate courses are taken, courses will transfer correctly, and prerequisite chains remain intact.
Required courses
WB 251 Project Management/Business Project (
BUS 3400
equivalent)
2 classes (6 credits) in humanities, social sciences, or communication areas (would meet Raider Core CLOs)
7 other business electives that align with required major courses or major electives.
Speak with your MSOE academic advisor to verify the correct classes are chosen before travel.

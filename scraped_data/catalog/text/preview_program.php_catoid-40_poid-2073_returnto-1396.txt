Overview
A student enrolled in a degree program at MSOE may also earn a certificate in German. Upon completion of this course of study, students would have solid communication skills in German and a strong understanding of German culture and history. A graduate with a certificate in German would be attractive to business and/or industry in that they would possess the marketable skill of a language other than English. Total required credits vary from 6 to 12, depending upon the student’s German language proficiency upon entering this course of study. At least 6 credits must be completed at MSOE to earn the certificate in German.

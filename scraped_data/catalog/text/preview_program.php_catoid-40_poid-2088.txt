The following courses fulfill the Raider Core requirement for
Think Critically.
Consult Scheduler to determine what courses are offered in a given term.
An asterisk indicates if the course covers more than one CLO. If courses cover more than one CLO, students must choose which CLO they will apply to the Raider Core.
Courses with the
Think Critically
CLO that are not taught out of the Mathematics Department or Physics and Chemistry Department are only eligible for the Raider Core elective.
Courses may not be double-counted toward more than one Raider Core requirement.

Overview
In the modern computing world, the need for cybersecurity expertise continues to climb. The cybersecurity minor provides students with exposure to the technical skills necessary to design and engineer modern, resilient, cybersecure products. Through a strong focus on laboratory experiences and experiential learning, students will be exposed to the core foundational principles of cybersecurity and well as core technical concepts necessary to build modern systems. By completing this minor, students will add important skills that will better prepare them for a challenging and rewarding career with the ability to work in multiple cybersecurity related areas or to pursue advanced degrees and certifications.

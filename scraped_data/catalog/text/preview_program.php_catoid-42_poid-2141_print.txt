Overview
The management minor allows students to complement their non-business degree with classes that will help them navigate through their career journey. Students pursuing any business major (Accounting, Finance, Management, Marketing, Operations & Systems Management, Technical & Business Management), are not eligible to complete a management minor. For additional information, see the
minors policy.

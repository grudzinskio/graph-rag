Overview
MSOE’s strong technical focus and industry driven curriculum provides a broad-based exploration of Operations and Systems Management. It delves deeply into supply chain management, operations management, enterprise resources planning (ERP), continuous improvement, and project management to provide students with a unique blend of technical skills, business knowledge and practical hands-on experiences. The program prepares students to be innovative collaborators with the power to leverage strategic thinking and technology to improve operating efficiency.
Student Program Outcomes
Graduates with a B.S. in Operations and Systems Management will be able to:
Strategically analyze, evaluate, and solve business problems using technology and an understanding of the social context
Prepare and deliver concise, persuasive stakeholder-centered information in a professional manner
Develop leaders of character who promote effectiveness in working with and leading diverse teams
Exhibit an entrepreneurial mindset
Evaluate a continuous improvement project using appropriate technology & analysis tools

Operations and Systems Management, B.S., THL Exchange Program Version S1A
This map is taken by MSOE students in the Operations and Systems Management major who enroll at Technische Hochschule Lübeck (THL) in Lübeck, Germany, for their third year. Students follow the regular Operations and Systems Management major path for their first, second, and fourth years.
Year Three (MSOE students at THL)
There are over 20 courses delivered in English to choose from at THL.
Students need to earn 30 semester credits (10 courses) to stay on track while at THL.
Students need to meet with their academic advisor
before
travel to ensure the appropriate courses are taken, courses will transfer correctly, and prerequisite chains remain intact.
Required courses
Supply Chain Management (
BUS 3890
equivalent)
Entrepreneurship (
BUS 3420

BUS 3400
equivalent)
Quantitative Methods in Business (
BUS 3010
equivalent)
1 class (3 MSOE credits) in humanities, social sciences, or communication areas (that would meet Raider Core CLOs)
4 other business electives that align with required major courses or major electives.
Speak with your MSOE academic advisor to verify the correct classes are chosen before travel.

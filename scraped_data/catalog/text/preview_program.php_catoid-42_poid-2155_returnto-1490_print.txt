Overview
The Minor in Mathematics is offered to those students who wish to expand their background and understanding of mathematics. A mathematics minor will strengthen a student’s specialty, make their degree more attractive to potential employers, and enhance the preparedness of the student for graduate school. For additional information, see the
minors policy.

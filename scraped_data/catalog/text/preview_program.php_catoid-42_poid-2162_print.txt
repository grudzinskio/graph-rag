Overview
The AI for Emerging Applications certificate is an interdisciplinary sequence of asynchronous course work, combined with a practicum experience that allows students from any major to develop a breadth of skills in the emerging realm of artificial intelligence (AI). Technical foundations will be gained in both AI and an application area, all while emphasizing the importance of human-centered design approaches to create ethically aligned, inclusive, and socially responsible AI. The practicum experience will provide a real-world industry-derived project. Students interested in this certificate should consult with the AI for Emerging Applications certificate coordinator to discuss project options and develop an appropriate course plan to provide relevant skills for the project. The student’s ability to enroll in the certificate program would depend on the availability and type of the practicum projects, and the student’s qualifications for these projects.

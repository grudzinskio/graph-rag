Overview
An electrical engineering minor complements many other engineering majors, providing a comprehensive circuits foundation and the option to gain depth in key topical electrical engineering areas. The EE minor requires 20 credits minimum, 8 required credits and then 12 credits from the list of approved courses. Students pursuing an electrical engineering major are not eligible to complete an electrical engineering minor. For additional information, see the
minors policy.

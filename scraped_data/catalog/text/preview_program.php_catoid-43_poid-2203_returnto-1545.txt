Overview
MSOE’s Graduate Certificate in Fluid Power is designed for recent graduates and professionals interested in developing a specialization in fluid power systems. The certificate program prepares students to develop in-depth engineering skills in the fields of hydraulics and controls. The expected completion time of the certificate is two semesters, six credits per semester. For additional information, see the
Certificates
policy.
Learning Outcomes
Upon completion of the certificate program, students will be able to:
Design and develop fluid power systems.
Analyze electrohydraulic components and systems.
Evaluate control strategies used in fluid power systems.

The AAS-EET to BSEE transfer plan at MSOE is designed to allow AAS graduates of electrical engineering technology programs from colleges with which MSOE holds transfer agreements, to transfer efficiently into the electrical engineering program. Benefits include:
An opportunity for AAS-EET graduates to earn the BSEE degree and advance to engineering positions in industry
Strong emphasis on design, applications and experienced-based learning
Transfer agreements with several two-year colleges
Full-time and part-time tracks
This program may also be appropriate for transfer students from other colleges. Consult with an academic advisor for more details and a list of colleges that have transfer agreements with MSOE.

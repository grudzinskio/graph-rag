Rising Raiders Application

The Rising Raiders Programs offers a week-long, residential, academic camps for students with a self-reported GPA of a 2.5 or higher and are interested in exploring our degrees. Registration for our week-long camp (July 19 – July 24, 2026) will have limited spots and will fill on a first come, first served basis in partnership with Milwaukee County high schools and our community partners.

For more information regarding scholarships or the application, please contact Edith Hernandez, Coordinator of Campus Inclusion Cohort Programs, at hernandeze@msoe.edu

Programs for Schools & Educators

Students explore the possibilities of STEM in and out of the classroom.

It’s never too early for students to learn about the endless opportunities within STEM. MSOE offers programming for students of all ages, including hands-on activities, workshops, competitions, conferences, and week-long camps. Find the program that meets your students’ needs.

All Ages Programs

STEM on Site

Groups may visit MSOE or we’ll bring MSOE to you. An MSOE STEM team member leads hands-on activities in our classroom or yours.

Learn More

Middle School Programs

Fluid Power Challenge

Middle school teachers are invited to bring their students to learn about and compete in fluid power with the MSOE Fluid Power Institute team and the National Fluid Power Association (NFPA).

Learn more

High School Programs

Opportunity Conference at MSOE

High school students compete against one another to show off their skills in computer science, science or math.

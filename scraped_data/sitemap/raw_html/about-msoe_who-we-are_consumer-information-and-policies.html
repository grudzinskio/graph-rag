MSOE Consumer Information Disclosures

Per the U.S. Higher Education Opportunity Act (HEOA) and other Federal regulations, MSOE must disclose information about the university, ranging from athletic, academic, financial aid, security, graduation rates, and more. As a means to making this “Students’ Right to Know” information readily available, MSOE has compiled this information arranged by topic. Paper copies of these consumer disclosures are available upon request.

Excellent Reputation and Rankings

Students come to MSOE because of its focus on laboratory experience and career practice, expert faculty dedicated to student learning, its small college feeling within a vibrant downtown neighborhood, extremely high placement rates for graduates and the success of its alumni.

MSOE is consistently ranked highly by a number of organizations, and while this is gratifying, it is important to note that rankings should not be overemphasized by students or their families during the college selection process. Choosing a college is an individual decision that should be made by students and families based on the student’s individual needs.

Here are some of the recognitions MSOE has received:

  • #6 Civil Engineering Program in the U.S. (tie)

  • #212 in Undergraduate Computer Science Program in the U.S. (tie)
  • #214 Bachelor of Science in Nursing Program in the U.S. (tie)
  • #2 in Overall Rank – Regional Universities (Midwest) (tie)
  • #4 in Best Value Schools – Regional Universities (Midwest)
  • #1 in Best Colleges for Veterans – Regional Universities (Midwest)
  • #2 in Most Innovative Schools – Regional Universities (Midwest)
  • #7 in Undergraduate Teaching Programs – Regional Universities (Midwest) (tie)
  • #41 in Top Performers in Social Mobility – Regional Universities (Midwest) (tie)

  • Best Colleges in the U.S. (#185 out of 584) (2026)

Rader School of Business

Rader School of Business

Smart business students know a good investment when they see one, and an MSOE business degree delivers an exceptional ROI.

 

The Rader School of Business has offered business and management degrees for more than 60 years. Our unique blend of business and technical courses — plus real-world experience gained through internships and innovative laboratories — will give you a competitive edge in today’s dynamic business environment. Our programs emphasize the understanding and application of current technology and the business practices essential for success in the 21st century.  

The school’s offices are located in Rosenberg Hall and may be reached at (414) 277-7279.

Undergraduate Degrees

Bachelor of Science in Accounting

Learn accounting and finance methods, paradigms, and techniques to become a technical leader on projects integrating accounting into business solutions.

Learn more

Bachelor of Science in Finance

Courses in advanced accounting and finance, management control systems, investment and portfolio analysis, and tax will prepare you for a career in finance and managing a department, division, or business.

Learn more

Bachelor of Science in Management

Gain a broad background in business through this all-encompassing degree program with balanced electives across operations, technology, marketing, management and finance.

Learn more

Bachelor of Science in Marketing

Through courses and real-world projects, you’ll develop marketing skills and learn about market research, advertising strategies, and consumer behavior to identify new customer opportunities and brand plans that contribute to the bottom line.

Learn more

Bachelor of Science in Operations and System Management

Develop the skillsets to become a leader and innovative collaborator with the power to leverage strategic thinking to improve operating efficiency.

Learn more

Bachelor of Science in Technical and Business Management (AAS to B.S. Transfer Program)

The AAS to B.S. in Technical and Business Management program prepares students to transfer seamlessly to MSOE to expand their technical expertise and work in a business-to-business sales career.

Learn more

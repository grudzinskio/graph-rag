User Experience Industrial Advisory Committee

User Experience Industrial Advisory Committee (UX IAC)

The User Experience (UX) Industrial Advisory Committee’s (IAC) purpose is to provide guidance to the UX program on industry trends and needs—ensuring the program produces industry-ready UX professionals.

The IAC helps to keep the curriculum up-to-date to maintain program goals and competencies. To do this, members of the committee are asked to weigh in on and vote to support or reject any proposed changes to the UX program.

View the committee bylaws.

2025–26 Members

The UX IAC is comprised of industry professionals, MSOE UX faculty and students. The membership reflects a balance of high-tech, finance, retail, consulting and start-up companies that are located locally, nationally and internationally.

Committee Information

The UX IAC meets formally, twice a year (December/May). In addition to the formal meetings, one-on-one conversations with the committee chair take place prior to the meetings to review materials and prepare for the formal meetings. For more information about the committee or if you have any questions, please contact Ash Dzick.

Fall Move-In: Pre-Season Athletes

  • Fall Pre-Season Athlete Move-In (General) - Tuesday, Aug. 18 (noon to 4 p.m.)
  • Fall Pre-Season Athlete Move-In (Women's Golf) - TBD (noon to 4 p.m.)

We are excited to welcome you to MSOE. We want to make the move-in day as smooth as possible for everyone.

  • Students may have up to 3 people help them move in. Once move-in is complete, guests are asked to leave the building.
  • Simply follow the steps below, and moving in can be as easy as 1-2-3!

Step 1: Know Your Hall, Room Number and Move-In Time.

Make sure you know which residence hall and room you are in. You can find your housing placement information on your my.msoe.edu. 

Step 2: Check In!

  • Viets Tower residents: ground floor front desk of Viets Tower, 1121 N. Milwaukee St.
  • Mellowes Hall residents: ground floor front desk of Mellowes Hall, 1121 N. Milwaukee St.
  • Margaret Loock Hall residents: ground floor front desk of MLH, 324 E. Juneau Ave.
  • Grohmann Tower residents: ground floor front desk of Grohmann Tower, 233 E. Juneau Ave.
  • Juneau Village Tower residents: leasing office, 1019 N Jackson St.

After you unload your vehicle and move it to a parking lot, you will pick up your room key at the front desk. You may park in the Water Street Lot or State Street Lot A to clear the unloading area.

Step 3: Unload!

Carts will be available to help move personal belongings. To ensure access for all, please use only one cart per family. After you’re finished, return the cart to the front desk and move your vehicle. You may park in the Water Street Lot or State Street Lot A to clear the unloading area.

Enjoy the rest of your summer, and we look forward to seeing you on campus soon! If you have any questions about move-in, housing, or the meal plan, please contact Living at MSOE.

Fall Move-In: Returning Students

We are excited to welcome you back to MSOE and want to make move-in day as smooth as possible for everyone. Simply follow the steps listed below, and moving in can be as easy as 1-2-3! If you have any questions about move-in, housing, or the meal plan, please contact Living at MSOE.

Returning Resident Move-In Schedule:

  • Sunday, Aug. 30, 2026: Between 8 a.m. and 3 p.m.

If you have a conflict with the above dates, please contact Living at MSOE.

Step 1: Know Your Hall, Room Number and Move-In Time.

Make sure you know which residence hall and room you are in. You can find this information on your my.msoe.edu. The front desk will have a listing of all residents and assigned rooms.

Step 2: Check In!

  • Viets Tower residents: ground floor front desk of Viets Tower, 1121 N. Milwaukee St.
  • Mellowes Hall residents: ground floor front desk of Mellowes Hall, 1121 N. Milwaukee St.
  • Margaret Loock Hall residents: ground floor front desk of Loock Hall, 324 E. Juneau Ave.
  • Grohmann Tower residents: ground floor front desk of Grohmann Tower, 233 E. Juneau Ave.
  • Juneau Village Tower residents: leasing office, 1019 N. Jackson St.

You will pick up your room key at the front desk. You may park in the Water Street Lot or State Street Lot A to clear the unloading area.

Step 3: Unload!

Carts will be available to help move personal belongings, except at JVT. To ensure access for all, please use only one cart per family. You may park in the Water Street Lot or State Street Lot A to clear the unloading area.

Enjoy the rest of your summer, and have a safe trip to campus!

What to Bring

Please see the full list of things to bring with you!

  • Loft rentals (optional for MLH Residence Hall only) are available at collegeproducts.com/msoe. Order by Aug. 1 to have your items delivered to your room before the New Student Move-In day.
  • You may want to coordinate with your roommate if you plan to share a television, a futon, an area rug, or other items.
    • Many recommend a 5'x7' Area Rug to place between your beds.
    • An 80-inch wide futon could fit in the residential rooms.
    • Review a complete list of Residence Life FAQs.

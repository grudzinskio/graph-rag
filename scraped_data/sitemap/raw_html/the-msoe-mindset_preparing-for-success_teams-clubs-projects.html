Teams, Clubs & Projects

Creating connections and building character

Meet Sean Ali

Life beyond the classroom is a critical part of your time at MSOE. Meeting new people and becoming an active part of the university community will not only kick-start your social life, but it may also enhance your resume through leadership development opportunities. MSOE offers more than 100 student organizations, enabling students develop existing passions while exploring new interests. From service organizations to honor societies, and student government to extreme sports, you'll find plenty of ways to be an engaged member of the MSOE community.

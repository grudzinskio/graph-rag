Academic Year Opportunities
To qualify for student employment during the academic year you are required to take at least six undergraduate credits or three graduate credits during each term of the academic year.
Positions designated as "Work-Study" are only available to students who have been awarded federal Work-Study.
Positions that do not indicate they are Work-Study positions are available to any interested MSOE student.

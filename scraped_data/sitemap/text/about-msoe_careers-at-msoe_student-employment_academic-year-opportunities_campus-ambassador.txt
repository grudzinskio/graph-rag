Campus Ambassador
Department: Campus Life
Job Description
You’ll help shape the MSOE experience by supporting or leading programs, initiatives, events, and campus traditions in Campus Life.
Between Welcome Week, student org collaboration, weekend programming, and more, you’ll strengthen your interpersonal skills, boost your leadership, develop your managerial skills, and increase your knowledge of MSOE resource and offices.
Looking for creative, curious, and innovative minds who are eager to enhance the MSOE student experience!

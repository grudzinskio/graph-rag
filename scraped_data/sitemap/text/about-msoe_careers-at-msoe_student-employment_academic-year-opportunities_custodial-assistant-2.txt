Custodial Assistant - Events
Department: Custodial Services
2024-2025 Academic Year
Do you enjoy working with other students and take pride in seeing a clean campus? If so, the Custodial Services department is hiring student employees to work throughout campus. Primary hours will be on weekends with some weekday availability.
Brief Job Description
Daily routine cleaning tasks
Detail cleaning of all surfaces
Use of all custodial equipment
Lift 25 pounds unassisted
Work independently as well as part of a team
Timely, follow directions, meet deadlines
Event set – up, portering, clean- up, tear – down
Must be able to work weekends and all major events
Other duties as assigned to maintain a clean and safe campus

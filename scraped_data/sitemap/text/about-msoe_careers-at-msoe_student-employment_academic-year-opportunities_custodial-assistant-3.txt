Custodial Assistant - Residence Halls
Custodial Assistant
Department: Custodial Services
2024-2025 Academic Year
Job Description
Hiring several enthusiastic part time student employees for work in MLH, Viets Tower and Regents Hall. Hours available during the time frame of 6:00 am - 6:00 pm Sunday through Saturday. Full time hours are available during breaks and over the Holidays.
Job duties include the following:
Detailed cleaning of bathrooms/shower areas, re-stocking supplies, clean common areas, stairwells
Empty trash and recycle
Work in a team environment
Lift 25 lbs
Follow directions.
Wages determined by Human Resources.

Environmental Health and Safety Assistant
Department: EHS
Disclaimer: Student employment is only available to MSOE students. To qualify for student employment during the academic year, you are required to be registered for at least six undergraduate credits or three graduate credits during each term of the academic year.
Develop practical skills in Environmental, Health, and Safety by assisting MSOE's Director of EHS. Responsibilities include collecting and processing e-waste, conducting OSHA/EPA compliance inspections (Lock-out Tag-out, equipment, general safety), maintaining and cleaning out campus workspaces, and participating in comprehensive safety training.

RCAS Tutor
Department: Raider Center for Academic Success
2025-2026 Academic Year
Job Description
RCAS tutors will tutor students in scheduled weekly meetings in a 1-1, small group, and drop-in setting. Tutors must have a “B” or better in any course that they wish to tutor. All tutors will provide excellent customer service in their interactions with students and become role models as they help their students find academic success at MSOE.

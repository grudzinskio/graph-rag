SHIP Shuttle Driver
SHIP Operator
Department: Public Safety
2025-2026 Academic Year
Job Description
The SHIP Operator operates an MSOE vehicle and transports MSOE students, faculty, and staff to various locations within the SHIP boundary. SHIP operates on Fridays and Saturdays between the hours of 5:00pm and 2:00am. The SHIP Operator will be required to take a 30-min unpaid break between 9:00pm and 9:30pm.

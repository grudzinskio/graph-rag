Summer Museum Associate
Summer Museum Associate
Department: Grohmann Museum
Summer 2026
Job Description:
The Grohmann Museum is home to the world's most comprehensive art collection dedicated to the evolution of human work and welcomes thousands of visitors per year. Museum Associates assist with front desk operations and work alongside the museum's professional staff to ensure a positive visitor experience for the MSOE community and general public.
Job Responsibilities:
Greet patrons and promote a positive visitor experience.
Check in events and tours.
Help manage events and K-12 programming for the summer months.
Conduct sales and inventory in the museum store.
Walk through the museum to monitor safety and cleanliness.
Complete other tasks as assigned by supervisor.
Qualifications:
Federal work study candidates are encouraged to apply
Must work well with a team.
Must possess great communication skills.
The ideal candidate will be comfortable in customer facing roles.
An interest in giving tours and assisting with public programs is a plus.
Hours:
The Museum is open 9:00am-5:00pm on weekdays, 12:00pm-6:00pm on Saturdays, and 1:00pm-4:00pm on Sundays. We are closed on major holidays. Applicants with
weekend availability
are encouraged to apply.

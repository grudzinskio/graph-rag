Campus Inclusion
The Office of Campus Inclusion helps make MSOE a place where everyone belongs and can fully participate. We develop and sustain educational programs to equip students with valuable skills for college and the workforce. We also build strong partnerships within the community to offer academic and social opportunities for all students to engage in STEM education. Additionally, we provide resources and guidance to help students build academic resilience and navigate challenges with confidence. Our goal is to ensure students not only thrive at MSOE but also achieve lasting success in their future careers, while feeling supported by an inclusive community.
Cohort Scholars engage in a seminar course and events on and off campus, building skills in academic resiliency, career exploration, community engagement, and leadership, while receiving personalized mentorship to support their growth.
This 10-week program is designed to pair admitted High School Seniors to collaborate with MSOE seniors as part of their capstone project.
Admitted Student Receptions invite prospective students and their families in the Spring to connect with current MSOE students, alumni, and professors.
The Rising Raiders Scholars Program is a four-year cohort-based program focused on academic exploration, academic success, math preparation skills and MSOE’s Eight Dimensions of Wellness.

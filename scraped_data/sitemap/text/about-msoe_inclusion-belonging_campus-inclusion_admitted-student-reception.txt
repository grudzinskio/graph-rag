Admitted Student Reception
These receptions offer students a chance to connect with new classmates and engage with MSOE’s president, alumni, faculty, and members of the Student Council for Campus Belonging. Guests can look forward to great food, fun activities, and exciting giveaways, making it a memorable start to their MSOE experience.

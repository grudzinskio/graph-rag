MSOE Senior Design Scholars Program Application
The Senior Design Scholars (SDS) Program at MSOE offers high school seniors a unique opportunity to engage in real-world, collegiate-level design projects by joining senior design teams during their final semester of high school. Gain hands-on experience working alongside MSOE students in fields such as biomedical, electrical, computer, mechanical, civil and architectural engineering, as well as construction management.

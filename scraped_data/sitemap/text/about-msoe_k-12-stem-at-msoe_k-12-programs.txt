K-12 Programs
Every year, thousands of K–12 students visit MSOE to take part in a variety of programs and competitions designed to foster and continue students’ excitement for the STEM fields. Specialized programming provides opportunities for elementary, middle and high school students to explore STEM through exciting, hands-on activities. MSOE offers evening and weekend programs, residential and non-residential summer camps, group activities, competitions and more to get students excited about STEM early on.
Bring your group or classroom to the STEM Center for hands-on learning and activities.
Middle and high school students are invited to showcase their STEM skills through annual events.
Programs are added throughout the year - be sure to sign up for program alerts to learn when new programs are available. Summer programs are posted by early February annually.

Opportunity Conference at MSOE
A one-of-a-kind competition for high school students to show off their skills in computer science and math.
*
At this time, we will only be able to accept 4 teams per school to register. If, however, you have additional teams, you may submit their information to us via email to be placed on a waitlist, and we will inform teachers/coaches of spots opening over the coming weeks. All spots will be given on a first-come, first-served basis.
Computer Competition
Friday, November 21st, 2025
Click Here to Register
Registration Fee: $85.00 per team; Teams registered at least 3 weeks before the competition are guaranteed t-shirts
Team registration may ONLY be done by your teacher / team coach using the online shopping cart.
Composition of team: four (4) students.
Teams are encouraged to bring their own laptops. Each team of four students will use two laptops.
Maximum number of teams the competition will host: 45
Be sure to review the
Computer Competition rules and instructions
.
Each four-member computer team works together using two laptops to design programs to solve problems of varying levels of difficulty. Teams may use either Python, Java (Eclipse or BlueJ) or C++ (Microsoft Visual Studio .NET). Participants are encouraged to bring wired or USB mice.
*We are unable to guarantee shirts for any teams who orders submitted after these dates. Thanks!

Programs for Families
Explore STEM with project-based learning experiences
Exploratory programs for elementary through high school students allow for project-based learning on a college campus. Programs are offered throughout the school year and in the summer to fit your family's busy schedule. A variety of STEM programs provide endless opportunities to learn in a fun, hands-on environment.

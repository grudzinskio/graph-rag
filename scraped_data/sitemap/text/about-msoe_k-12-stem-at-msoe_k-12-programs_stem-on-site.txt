STEM on Site
Overview
We are currently accepting requests for the 2025-2026 academic year. Programs are available between 9 a.m. - 3 p.m. Monday through Friday, September through May.
STEM on Site programs are an opportunity for students to explore a hands-on STEM activity beyond their classroom curriculum. STEM on Site is not designed to replace a full school STEM curriculum and is recommended to be implemented as a single or small series of programs throughout a grade band in a school.
This program is available on a first come, first served basis pending staff availability.
If you are a high school group looking for a campus tour only, please refer to our
admissions page.
Support for this program is made possible through funding from AGC Foundation, American Family Insurance, Brady Foundation, Eaton, Northwestern Mutual and Rockwell Automation.

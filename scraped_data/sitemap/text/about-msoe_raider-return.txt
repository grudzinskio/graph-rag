COVID-19/Raider Return
MSOE continually monitors all local and national health alerts and is prepared to make adjustments to operations if needed.
The federal COVID-19 Public Health Emergency Declaration ended in May 2023. As a result, MSOE:
No longer requires quarantine protocols or contact tracing.
No longer requires students, faculty or staff to report positive cases and systems.
No longer tracks vaccination status.
If you are experiencing upper respiratory symptoms, please follow the
CDC guidance on Preventing Spread of Respiratory Viruses
.
Health Services offers health resources and referrals to assist students during the academic year.

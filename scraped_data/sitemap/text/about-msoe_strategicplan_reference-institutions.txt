Reference Institutions
MSOE utilizes a group of respected universities to benchmark our performance. By comparing short and long-term indicators, independent rankings and
MSOE’s own KPI’s
to these institutions, we are able to measure and report our success or identify areas for continuous improvement.  It’s important to note that MSOE is a nationally respected and truly distinct university making direct comparisons to other institutions difficult. Any comparisons are carefully weighted using multiple, individual factors that are directly relevant to MSOE’s strategic plan strategies, actions and objectives.

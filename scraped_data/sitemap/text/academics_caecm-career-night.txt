The
Civil and Architectural Engineering and Construction Management Department’s
Annual Career Night provides employers an opportunity to showcase their company, meet MSOE faculty and students, and recruit students for internships and full-time employment.
This event is the perfect opportunity to meet and network in-person with students enrolled in our civil engineering, architectural engineering and construction management programs. It's a must attend for any company interested in filling a talent pipeline.
Each year we are excited to welcome a wide variety of companies to campus.
Our interactive campus map will help you make your way to and around campus.
For a list of additional events, please visit the Career Connections website.

Shipping Information
If your booth display is being shipped to MSOE via FedEx, UPS or USPS, please direct delivery to this address:
MSOE CAECM Career Night
1000 N. Market Street
Milwaukee, WI 53202
PLEASE ensure you add your company name and CAECM Career Night to the outside of your package.
Because of our very limited storage space, PLEASE send packages to arrive in the following time frame only: Monday, November 4 – Thursday, November 7, 2024.

All return shipments must be properly packaged, including fully completed carrier shipping labels with your organization's shipping account number. Carrier pickup is located at MSOE Shipping and Receiving: 1000 N. Market Street, Milwaukee, WI 53202.

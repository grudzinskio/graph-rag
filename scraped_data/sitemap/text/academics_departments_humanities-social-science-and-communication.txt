Humanities, Social Science and Communication Department
Our mission is to cultivate critical thinkers and effective communicators who will address global issues, foster cultural understanding, and lead with empathy and self-awareness in an interconnected world.
Our department offers a variety of courses in the humanities, social sciences, English, communication and user experience — courses designed to develop and enrich your mind so you become more sensitive to and fully aware of yourself and others.
The department’s administrative office is located on the second floor of the
Grohmann Museum
and may be reached at (414) 277-7374.

Mathematics Department
Become fluent in the universal language of mathematics.
The Mathematics Department is home to the actuarial science major, which was recently ranked the second best in the nation by College Choice, an independent college search and rankings website. The department also offers the mathematics minor and a variety of required and elective courses to support other programs at MSOE.
The department is located on the
third floor of the Walter Schroeder Library
and may be reached at (414) 277-7452.
For information about math placement, visit the
MSOE ALEKS Mathematics Placement Assessment webpage

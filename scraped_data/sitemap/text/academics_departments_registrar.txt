The Registrar’s Office staff is available to assist you Monday through Friday from 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
Many forms can be sent to us via email. If you have questions about specific forms, please contact our office. Some release forms require us to verify that you (the student) signed it. If you fill out the form and send it electronically, we will follow up with you for a quick Teams call with you to verify that it came from you.

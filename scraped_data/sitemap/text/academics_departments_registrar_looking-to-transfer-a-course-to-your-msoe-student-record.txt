Looking to transfer a course to your MSOE student record?
Step 1: Transferology account
Do you have a Transferology account?  If no, follow instructions
here
.
Step 2: Equivalency search
Review the courses you are intending to take for equivalency through Transferology.  Detailed instructions can be found
here
.  Note, you can also enter in progress courses.  MSOE only will transcribe courses with a grade of C or higher.  The transfer credit policy can be found below.
Step 3: Notification - Transfer Credit Notification Form
If you intend to take a course outside of MSOE, please fill out the
Transfer Credit Notification Form
.  Even if you have identified that your intended course has established equivalency at MSOE, this form is extremely important.  This will aid your professional advisor and the Registrar's office in preparing your academic record for subsequent term registration.
Step 4: Final Transcript
Once the intended course is completed, please send final transcript to MSOE - Registrar's Office.

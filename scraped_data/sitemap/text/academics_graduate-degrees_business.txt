Graduate Degrees in the Rader School of Business
Graduate programs offered through the Rader School of Business feature relevant, applications-oriented course work taught by expert faculty. Our blended Internet courses mean you’ll get the best of both worlds—convenient scheduling and valuable face-time with faculty and other students. The MSOE MBA and M.S. in Engineering Management both offer specializations in Advanced Business Strategy using AI and Analytics, and New Product Management. The MBA program also offers a specialization in Education Leadership.
If you’re a professional working for a company or organization driven by technology, the MSOE MBA is designed for you. Built upon more than 60 years of tradition preparing engineers and technical professionals for advancement to leadership careers, the MSOE MBA serves students from a variety of industry backgrounds.
The MBA in Education Leadership program prepares leaders to become outstanding principals and school leaders. It combines education leadership with a business/entrepreneurial mindset – for those ready to enact transformational change at the individual, school and district level. This is a different kind of program for a different kind of leader.
Often called “The Engineer’s MBA,” a master’s degree in engineering management combines your technical background with a core business curriculum. With the choice to take classes 100% online or through a blended-Internet delivery format, the program provides flexibility.

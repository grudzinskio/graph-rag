Graduate Engineering Degrees
Whether you have set your sights on a professional engineering license, or just want to deepen your knowledge in a specific discipline, MSOE can help you take the next step in your professional life with a master's degree in one of our four graduate programs.

In each program, students choose an area of focus. Architectural engineering students emphasize either structural engineering or mechanical-electrical-plumbing (MEP). Civil engineering students pick from construction management, environmental and water resources engineering, transportation engineering, or structural engineering. Broad-based engineering students hone in on either mechanical or electrical disciplines.
All four programs offer the flexibility to enroll part-time or full-time to meet the demands of a working professional.
Reach the next level of expertise with a master’s degree in Architectural Engineering. You’ll enjoy convenient scheduling and one-on-one interaction with expert faculty who bring their real-world experience to the classroom.

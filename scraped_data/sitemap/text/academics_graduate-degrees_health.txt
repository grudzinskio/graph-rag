Enhance your credentials and grow your career with a graduate degree from MSOE.
Health care is one of the fastest growing occupations in the economy. MSOE's health-related graduate programs will help position you for professional advancement in this rapidly growing field. MSOE offers master's degrees in two health-related fields:
Nursing Leadership and Management
Perfusion
All MSOE graduate students are eligible to apply for the Federal Direct Graduate PLUS Loan. Most of MSOE’s graduate students are employed full-time and over 70% receive employer tuition support. If you are employed, we also recommend that you speak with your employer about tuition reimbursement assistance.
MSOE offers two options in its MSN program: the business-oriented Leadership and Management Track and a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner Track for those seeking to provide mental health care.
At MSOE, we’ve been educating perfusionists since 1983. Students benefit from our expert faculty members, extensive clinical training and thorough course work.

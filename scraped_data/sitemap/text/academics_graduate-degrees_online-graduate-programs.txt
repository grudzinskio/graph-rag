Online Graduate Programs
Advance your career with an online master's in machine learning, certificate in machine learning, certificate in advanced business strategy using AI and analytics, or online MBA from Milwaukee School of Engineering.
Grow your expertise in machine learning and AI concepts with an online master's in machine learning or an online graduate certificate in applied machine learning.
MSOE’s Online MBA offers a flexible, 100% online experience designed for working professionals. Choose from a general MBA or specializations in AI & Analytics, New Product Management, or Education Leadership.

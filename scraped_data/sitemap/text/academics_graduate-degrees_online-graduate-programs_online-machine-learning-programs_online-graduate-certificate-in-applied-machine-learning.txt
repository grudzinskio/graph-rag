Online Graduate Certificate in Applied Machine Learning
Lead AI and machine learning projects in 9 months with MSOE's online graduate certificate.
This online certificate provides a strong foundation in Data Science, Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning, and artificial intelligence (AI) with an emphasis on real-world applications. Upon completion, students will be able to design, implement, and evaluate AI-driven solutions and be positioned to apply data science, machine learning, and AI in their professional work.
Key Program Features
Rapid Skill Development:
Complete the program and gain practical AI and machine learning skills in as little as nine months.
Industry Focus:
Blends practical skills and theory, designed to meet employer demand for AI leaders.
Curriculum:
Two application-oriented courses
that merge statistics and computer science to create prediction and decision-making algorithms.
Learning:
Hands-on projects guided by experienced faculty.
Career Impact:
Prepare to
lead ML and AI projects
and spearhead technology adoption in your organization.
Who Should Apply?
Experienced professionals and recent graduates looking to elevate their careers.
Ideal candidates typically have:
A bachelor's degree in a technical field (e.g., Computer Science, Electrical Engineering).
At least one year of college-level programming coursework or professional software development experience.
At least one year of differential calculus.
Key Details & Benefits
Duration:
Complete the program in 9 months (two semesters, one course per semester).
Pace:
Accelerated—builds quickly on your existing technical background.
Faculty:
Learn directly from expert, student-oriented faculty with AI expertise
.
Advancement:
Credits earned can be applied toward the Master of Science in Machine Learning
program.
Unique Combination:
Combine new expertise in Data Science, ML, and AI
with your current
domain knowledge
.

Program Details & Learning Outcomes
This program prepares you to collect, analyze, develop, and deploy ML and AI solutions:
Data Handling
: Collect, prepare, visualize data, and perform statistical analyses.
Modeling
: Develop and evaluate new predictive models, including deep learning. Improve model prediction performance using AI and advanced problem-solving techniques.
Application
: Apply machine learning and AI to diverse data types (spreadsheets, images, text, sensor readings, etc.).
Deployment
: Design solutions for complex technical problems, ranging from resource-constrained embedded systems to large-scale web services.
Communication
: Communicate results and actionable insights effectively to non-technical audiences.

Program Features
Grow Your Knowledge. Strengthen Your Career.
Connect theory to application with MSOE’s Graduate Certificate in Applied Machine Learning. You will take one course per semester, giving you the time and focus to master the concepts being taught by our expert faculty members. Through hands-on learning and in-depth projects, you will complete the program with high-value skillsets that employers need to drive business forward. Explore the curriculum.
Program Tuition & Financial Aid
Total estimated tuition: $12,800*
The online Graduate Certificate in Applied Machine Learning is 8 credit hours in total, with 4 credit hours earned each semester.
We understand that financing your graduate education is a critical step. We are here to help.
Financial Resources and Support:
Payment Plans:
MSOE offers convenient monthly payment plans.
Employer Tuition Assistance:
Many employers provide benefits through qualified educational assistance programs. This is a valuable option, as you may be able to utilize up to $5,250 of employer benefits to help finance your continuing education. We encourage you to check with your employer for eligibility.

MSOE’s Online MBA program is rooted in applied learning. Choose from a general MBA track, or optional specializations in analytics or new product management. Develop strategic leadership and decision making skills through a tech-focused lens.
The Online MBA in Education Leadership is the first program of its kind in the U.S., combining a rigorous MBA curriculum with unique courses tailored to school principals and aspiring school leaders. This program is a pathway to principal licensure in Wisconsin.

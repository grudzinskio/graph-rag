Hands on Future
Get ready for a day of discovery, innovation, and fun at MSOE!
High school students are invited to join us on campus Jan. 19, 2026 for Hands on Future, hosted by MSOE and MSOE's Society of Women Engineers (SWE). This immersive experience offers students a firsthand look at what it's like to be an engineer at MSOE. Participants will have the opportunity to:
Dive into hands-on activities across a variety of engineering disciplines
Connect with current MSOE students and professors
Explore different majors and learn what makes MSOE unique
Registration is closed.

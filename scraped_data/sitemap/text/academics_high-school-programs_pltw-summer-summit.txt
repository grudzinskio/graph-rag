MSOE Explore Summit for PLTW Students
The 2026 MSOE Explore Summit for PLTW Students will be held July 12 - July 17, 2026!
REGISTRATION FOR THE 2026 SUMMIT WILL OPEN FEBRUARY 1, 2026.
The MSOE Explore Summit for PLTW Students is an opportunity for students to spend the week rotating through half-day programs to learn about each of MSOE’s engineering majors and determine which one interests them the most. Two majors will be introduced each day through hands-on curriculum. Majors include architectural, biomedical, biomolecular, civil, computer, electrical, mechanical and software engineering. The week will also feature key PLTW sponsors and MSOE corporate partners.
*Registration spots will be awarded on a first-come, first-serve basis. To register, students must be nominated by their high school PLTW teacher. Additional information and the registration link will be shared with nominated students by their high school instructor. A $50 deposit must be submitted at the time of registration to reserve your spot, which is refundable at completion of the Summit.

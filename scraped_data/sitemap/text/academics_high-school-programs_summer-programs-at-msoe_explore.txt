Explore Summer Programs
MSOE’s 2026 High School Summer Programs will be offered in July. We hope you can join us this summer!
Explore camps are an opportunity for students to spend the week rotating through half-day programs to learn either about each of MSOE’s engineering majors or an array of healthcare disciplines, allowing students to determine which interests them the most.
Explore Summer Programs:
Two majors will be introduced each day through hands-on curriculum. Majors include: construction management, and architectural, biomedical, chemical & biomolecular, civil, computer, electrical, mechanical, and software engineering.

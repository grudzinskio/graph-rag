Focus on Health Professions Summer Program
The Focus on Health Professions Summer Programs are a collaborative effort between MSOE and the Medical College of Wisconsin (MCW) providing an opportunity for students to explore a variety of healthcare programs offered by each.
As students rotate through an array of healthcare disciplines, getting some hands-on experiences, they will be able to determine which area of is of interest to them.  Areas of focus may include: public health, global health, pharmacy, nursing and more.
Public Health
- learn about tackling real-world, community health challenge in both urban and rural settings.
Pharmacy-
learn about becoming a pharmacist of the future, providing a broader range of patient care services including innovation and advocacy for access to primary and preventative care.
Global Health
- learn about becoming a global health leader, using community engaged approaches to promote health equity.
Bioethics
– learn how to analyze and resolve difficult ethical issues that arise in health care institutions.
Clinical and Translational Science
– learn t how to translate research outcomes into care for the sick and to improve community health.
Nursing
- learn about the many roles and providing care in complex clinical situations at the bedside and in the community using compassion, critical thinking, technology and science.

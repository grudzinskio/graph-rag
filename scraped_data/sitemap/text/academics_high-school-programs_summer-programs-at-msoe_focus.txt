Focus Summer Programs
MSOE’s 2026 High School Summer Programs are being offered in July. We hope you can join us!
Focus on the Possibilities is for high school students who are focused in on a particular major for their college education. Students come to spend a week at MSOE to focus on the interests that matter to them. Spend time in labs, meet with professors and interact with other students with similar interests. It is a great way to spend part of the summer!

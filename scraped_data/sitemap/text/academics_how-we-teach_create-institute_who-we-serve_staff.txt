CREATE is committed to building community through offering high quality programming that intentionally brings individuals from all corners of campus together.
MSOE is a community of learners dedicated to helping our students develop the mindset and skills needed to solve the diverse technical challenges of the 21st century. The CREATE Institute strives to foster an inclusive environment for organic collaboration.

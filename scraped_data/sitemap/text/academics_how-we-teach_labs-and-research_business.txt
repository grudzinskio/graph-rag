Business Labs & Facilities
At the Rader School of Business, you won’t just read about the technology and other tools that drive business — you’ll have the space to try them out, honing the technical and business skills you need to compete in today’s workplace.
Uihlein/Spitzer Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship: The Innovent Center
It’s not uncommon for MSOE students to develop new products, design new processes and uncover new markets. Some even launch their own businesses.
The Innovent Center
supports the entrepreneurial efforts of students and faculty, preparing them to join business and industry in leadership roles and improve profitability and productivity.

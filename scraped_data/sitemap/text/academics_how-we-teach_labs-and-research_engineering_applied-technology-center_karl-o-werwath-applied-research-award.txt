Karl O. Werwath Applied Research Award
Karl O. Werwath Applied Research Award
This annual award is granted to an MSOE faculty or staff member for their significant contribution in conducting applied research project(s) that benefitted or impacted business, industry and the community.
Award Overview
Applied research represents the use of scientific study and technical knowledge to solve the complex challenges facing society today. Types of applied research include:
Contribution to applied, engineering and scientific research and scholarship
Publications resulting from research
Patentable concepts
Karl O. Werwath, an innovator in engineering education and the application of technology, believed that teaching effectiveness was enhanced through applied research and consulting. He believed that MSOE should make an effort to contribute to advance technical knowledge for the benefit of business and industry for the good of the community and the nation. This award was initiated to recognize the vision of Karl O. Werwath and the contribution of MSOE faculty and staff who have fostered the advancement of applied scientific knowledge or promoted research at MSOE (including major collaboration).
Want to nominate someone for the 2025 Karl O. Werwath Applied Research Award? Please complete the online award nomination form.

FPI Independent Research Laboratories
Serving industry since 1962, FPI is one of the leading academic fluid power laboratories in the nation. Most projects are privately funded and are conducted in strict confidence.
What We Do
We perform many types of tests and analyses:
Burst tests
Impulse/fatigue tests
Performance tests
Environmental evaluations
Troubleshooting & failure analysis
Component manufacturing contamination
Advanced Projects
Component design and optimization using solid free-form fabrication
Computational fluid dynamics
Finite element analysis
Hydraulic pump and motor efficiency
Modeling and simulation of electrohydraulic systems
Noise and leakage control
Water jet research
New product research and design analysis
System design, construction and installation
Laboratory Facilities
We have specialized equipment for evaluating pumps, valves, motors, cylinders, tubes, filters, and winches in accordance with appropriate NFPA, ANSI, SAE, ISO and MIL standards. We can also build custom test stands and equipment for specialized machinery.
On Campus Facility
Our on-campus FPI laboratory occupies 2,400 square feet and features eight test cells with the following capabilities:
Test Cell 1: 150 HP, 65 gpm, 5,000 psi
Test Cell 2: 450 HP, 120 gpm, 6,500 psi
Test Cell 3: 200 HP, 65 gpm, 5,500 psi
Test Cell 4: 100 HP, 13.5 gpm, 15,000 psi
Test Cell 5 and 6: 50 HP, 8 gpm, 10,000 psi
Test Cell 6: 200 HP hydraulic motion testing
70,000 psi burst pressure
1,000 psi collapse chamber
Chase Avenue Facility
Our off campus laboratory facility, located in the Chase Commerce Center on South Chase Avenue in Milwaukee, is a reconfigurable 12,000 square foot workspace with drive-in access, a high-bay ceiling and major hydraulic power capabilities.
Our typical projects are estimated on a time and material basis. Written proposals include work scope, cost estimates, scheduling, and confidentiality agreements.

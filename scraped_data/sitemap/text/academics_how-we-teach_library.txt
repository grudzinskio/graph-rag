Walter Schroeder Library
The MSOE Library provides the resources and space you need to study, collaborate and research to enhance your educational and professional experiences.
Whether you enjoy studying alone or with a group, the Walter Schroder Library has spaces to research, study and collaborate. Visit us virtually or in person to connect with our friendly, knowledgeable librarians who are here to help you succeed.
Go beyond Google by using our resources to discover nearly 500,000 print and electronic books and access more than 100,000 electronic journals full of millions of scholarly articles.

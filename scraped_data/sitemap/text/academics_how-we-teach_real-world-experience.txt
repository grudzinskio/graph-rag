Real-World Experience
It’s the linchpin of an MSOE education and what sets our graduates apart.
It starts in
our labs
, where you’ll spend hundreds of hours practicing what you’ve learned using industry-standard equipment. Perhaps you’ll get involved in
undergraduate research
, working alongside faculty mentors to solve real-world problems, or perhaps you'll hold an internship at a local company, gathering valuable experience and connections before you graduate. You might even intern overseas through a
study abroad program
. By the time you start your first real job, you’ll have a huge head start over your peers and will be ready to wow your employer.
One of the most important investments you'll make is your education. An education from MSOE means direct entry into programs (you'll take classes towards your major on day one!), a four year graduation guarantee, learning in the heart of Wisconsin's tech hub (downtown Milwaukee!), application oriented and hands-on learning, and so much more. Now more than ever, MSOE is a S.M.A.R.T. decision.

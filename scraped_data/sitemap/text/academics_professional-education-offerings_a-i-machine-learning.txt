A.I. / Machine Learning
Expand your knowledge of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
The A.I. / Machine Learning certificates and programs provide diverse professionals and businesses with the cutting-edge knowledge and skills required to continuously advance expertise and maintain a competitive edge in the evolving field of artificial intelligence. These certificates and programs serve unique audiences to include those seeking an introduction and foundational knowledge of A.I., data scientists and machine learning enthusiasts involved in AI applications, and leaders focused on incorporating A.I. into their business strategy.
Each A.I. / Machine Learning Professional Certificate Program offers participants the opportunity to secure a skill-specific digital badge upon successful completion. Participants are not eligible to earn badges for webinars or non-certificate programs.
*All A.I. programming is offered online synchronously.
These A.I. / Machine Learning programs and webinars follow a fixed schedule with set class times, whether delivered in person or online. Learn live from our subject matter expert instructors and receive immediate feedback as you progress through the material with your peers as a group.
These A.I. / Machine Learning courses are online asynchronous, allowing you to complete the material on your own timeline. You can start whenever you want, spend as much or as little time on each aspect of the course as needed, and finish according to your personal schedule.

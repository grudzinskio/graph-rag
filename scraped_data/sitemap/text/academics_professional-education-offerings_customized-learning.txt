MSOE at Work: Consulting and Customized Training
Strategic Talent Development. Real-World Impact.
MSOE’s Center for Professional Education (CPE), through its
MSOE at Work
program, partners with organizations like yours to strengthen workforce capability through consulting, customized training, and applied learning experiences. Designed around your specific goals, our offerings deliver practical, skills-focused learning that employees can immediately apply on the job: driving performance, innovation, and long-term growth.
Customized Training Solutions
Every organization is different—and your training should be, too. We collaborate closely with your leadership and MSOE subject matter experts to design and deliver customized learning experiences aligned with your desired outcomes, operational priorities, and workforce needs. Trainings can be delivered on-site, online, or hybrid formats, with flexible content, scope, and scheduling to minimize disruption while maximizing impact.
Popular areas of focus across all industries include:
Applied Artificial Intelligence (A.I.)
– practical use cases, implementation strategies, and workforce readiness
New Technology Integration
– accelerating adoption and improving ROI on emerging technologies
Leadership & Supervisory Development
– building confident, effective leaders at all levels
Project Management
– tools, frameworks, and best practices for successful execution
Continuous Improvement & Workflow Optimization
– enhancing efficiency, quality, and performance
CPE Speakers Bureau
Bring MSOE expertise directly to your organization or event. Our Speakers Bureau connects your organization with experienced professionals for engaging 1–2-hour presentations, workshops, webinars, or conference sessions. Speakers draw from MSOE’s strengths in engineering, technology, business, and healthcare, offering credible, thought-provoking perspectives that resonate with technical and non-technical audiences alike.
Each session delivers:
Actionable insights grounded in real-world experience
Current industry trends and applied best practices
Practical takeaways your audience can use immediately
Consulting Services
When your organization faces strategic challenges or opportunities for transformation, we offer access to a deep bench of industry experts. Our consultants partner with your team to assess needs, identify opportunities, and develop practical, results-driven solutions. From short-term advisory support to longer-term engagements, our consulting services help organizations turn challenges into sustainable competitive advantages.

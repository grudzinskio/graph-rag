Electrical Principles and Practices I
Electrical Principles and Practices I
This 21-hour 4-day seminar is Part One of an introduction to electrical and electronic principles and practices and their uses in residential, commercial, and industrial applications. Chapters have been expanded to include greater coverage of personal protection and safety. A CD-ROM is included and contains information to supplement the textbook.

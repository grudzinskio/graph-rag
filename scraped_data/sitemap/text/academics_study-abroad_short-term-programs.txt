Short-Term Programs
You don’t have to commit to a full term to participate in global learning. Each year, there are a variety of opportunities for students to travel for one to two weeks. Faculty-led courses enable students to take a class at MSOE, earn credit and then travel with faculty members and classmates. Students can also have a hands-on experience by joining a student organization that teams with international non-profits to improve community infrastructure. There are always new and exciting places students are traveling.
For example, students might:
Travel to London after studying health care in the UK
Take a German
Art History class in Munich
Visit Cuba and study the country's history in Old Havana
Build a bridge in Guatemala with Engineers Without Borders
Set up a clinic in Nicaragua with
Global Brigades
Be immersed in the health care practices, traditions, and culture of Costa Rica

Manipal Institute of Technology
You could study abroad in one of the most rapidly developing countries in the world – India.
Manipal Institute of Technology
(MIT Manipal) and MSOE have signed exchange agreements for program(s) in the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Department.
However, the program is being reviewed and redesigned. We are not currently sending students to India until further notice.
MIT Manipal offers many undergraduate programs as well as seven graduate engineering degrees. Their laboratories are well equipped, and faculty members are experts in their field. Faculty members live on campus and welcome close interaction with students.
The 160-acre MIT Manipal campus is located along the western coastline of India in the state of Karnataka, just 150 miles south of the world-famous resort state Goa with its beautiful beaches.

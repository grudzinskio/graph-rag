Sohum and Sheetal Sohoni Study Abroad Award
Award Overview
This award has been created to support students pursuing a study abroad experience in a new country and culture, with a focus on intercultural learning, global engagement, and academic/personal growth.
Amount: Two $500 awards will be granted annually
Application Opens: February 1, 2026
Application Deadline: August 1, 2026
Recipients will be notified mid-August
Eligibility Criteria
Applicants must:
Be currently enrolled as a full-time MSOE undergraduate student
Have a 2.8 or higher cumulative GPA at the time of application
Have completed required documents to participate MSOE-approved summer, semester or year-long study abroad program (see question 10)
Not plan to study abroad in their home country
Applicants should also demonstrate:
A strong interest in global learning and intercultural engagement
Commitment to sharing their experience with the MSOE community upon their return
A clear financial need that would benefit from the award

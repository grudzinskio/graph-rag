Financial Aid Information
Most families have a genuine concern about affording a college education. That’s why MSOE is committed to assisting with the entire financial aid process.  From filling out your FAFSA to accepting your award and any steps in between, MSOE is here to help.
MSOE's Financial Aid Office is available to assist students and families with navigating the Financial Aid Process. We encourage you to explore the links below to get an understanding of how to apply for aid, the terms of scholarships, grants, and loans, and the additional financial aid opportunities that are available.

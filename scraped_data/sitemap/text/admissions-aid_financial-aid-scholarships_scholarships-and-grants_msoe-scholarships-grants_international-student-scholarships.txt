International Student Scholarships
The following are MSOE scholarships available to our international student population.
International Student Academic Scholarships
MSOE offers academic tuition-based scholarships to incoming international students based on their high school or transfer cumulative GPA. Academic scholarships are renewable for returning students on a yearly basis up to a maximum of six years (12 full-time terms) so long as they continue to be full-time and meet the
SAP policy
.
Phi Theta Kappa (PTK) Scholarship
Incoming transfer or international students who are current or past members of Phi Theta Kappa (PTK) are eligible to receive a tuition- based scholarship, renewable for up to six years (12 full-time terms).
Summer Programs Scholarship
Incoming freshmen and international students who have participated in MSOE’s Summer Programs during high school are eligible to receive a tuition-based scholarship. This tuition-based scholarship is renewable for up to six years (12 full-time terms) so long as students continue to be full-time and meet the
SAP policy.

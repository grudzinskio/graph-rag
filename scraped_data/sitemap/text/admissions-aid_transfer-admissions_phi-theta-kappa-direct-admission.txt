Phi Theta Kappa Direct Admission to MSOE
Your academic excellence deserves recognition. At MSOE, we proudly honor the achievement of Phi Theta Kappa (PTK) members. As a PTK student, you qualify for direct admission to MSOE as a transfer student, streamlining your path to a rigorous, career-focused education.
Fill out the inquiry form to get started with your PTK direct admission.

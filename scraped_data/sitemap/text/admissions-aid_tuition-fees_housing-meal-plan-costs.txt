Meal Plan Fees
Students will have access to an unlimited meal plan, allowing them to eat when they want and as much as they wish in MSOE’s
Robert Spitzer Dining Commons
. The unlimited meal plan is a robust program; students can enjoy as much or as little as they need to stay healthy and focused without worrying about when and how to dine.
Students who live in one of MSOE’s Residence Halls or on the Residence Hall floors of the Grohmann Tower will be required to take the meal plan. Using this plan, students eat in the Robert Spitzer Dining Commons when they want and as much as they wish from Monday–Sunday during operating hours. This plan is also available to students who live off-campus and can be purchased through a student's my.msoe portal.

Block Meal Plan
Residents who do not have an unlimited meal plan and students living off-campus can purchase meals in blocks on
my.msoe.edu
. Block plans are active for the academic year from purchase through the last day of the spring semester and do not roll over into the next academic year. Additional blocks can be purchased to increase the number of meals.
No refunds will be given for unused meals on the block plan.

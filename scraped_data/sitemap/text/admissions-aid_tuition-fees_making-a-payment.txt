Making a Payment
MSOE partners with Transact to make paying online quick and easy. Just select what you are paying to be redirected to the appropriate payment site.
Current students can view their billing statements and make payment by logging into their Transact account through single sign-on in my.msoe.edu.
International students can view their billing statements and make payment by logging into their Transact account through single sign-on in my.msoe.edu. Transact offers an International Fund Transfer payment option that can process payment with a student's native currency.
Pay your MSOE Parking citation online through Transact. Make sure to have your citation number and license plate number with you in order to submit payment.
Students not currently registered but have an outstanding balance can make payments online or mail in a check:

Students have the option to designate another party to view and pay student bills. This third party (Parents, family members, friends or employers) will need a one-time authorization, issued by the student via the payment website, Transact. The authorized user can then access the third party vendor system directly to view the student's billing information and make payments.
Accepted incoming student can make their $175 ($250 for graduate) enrollment deposit through our site, which secures their place in the incoming class. This deposit is also credited towards your first term tuition bill.
This payment link is for non-student rent and parking payments. Non-student is defined as summer interns, faculty and staff. Current MSOE student should log into their Transact account through mymsoe.edu to complete payment for housing.

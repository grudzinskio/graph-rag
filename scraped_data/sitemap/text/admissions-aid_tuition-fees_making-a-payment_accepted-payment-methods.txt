Accepted Payment Methods
The Milwaukee School of Engineering (MSOE) offers a variety of payment options, please see below:
In Person
In-person payments can be made in the Student Accounts Office - CC301 with
check only
(529's, cashier's check and money orders)
Online
Online payments can be made online through Transact using the following payment methods:
Credit / Debit Card
- Processing fees apply.
Checking/Saving Account (e-check)
- You will need your banking routing number as well as the account number for your checking or savings account. No processing fees applied.
Semester Payment Plan
- MSOE offers a semester payment plan option through Transact for the Fall and Spring terms.
529 Plans
- Transact supports direct payments from many common 529 savings plans. If your 529 plan is not listed among the available options, you can coordinate with your provider to have a check sent directly to MSOE.
International Funds Transfer
- MSOE is partnered with Transact Campus and Flywire for payment with your home currency.
Mail your payment
Mail personal checks or from your 529 plan provider made payable to Milwaukee School of Engineering or MSOE to the following address:

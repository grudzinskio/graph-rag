Other Fees
During their time at MSOE, students may incur additional fees beyond the standard mandatory charges, such as tuition, technology fees, student support fees, and housing/meals. These 'Other Fees' are conveniently added to their student account for clear viewing and seamless payment. Here are a few examples:
Parking Charges
Inclusive Access (online books/course materials)
Lab Parts
Books and Supplies purchased in the
Raider Shop
Club Sport Fees & Dues
Monthly Finance Charges on unpaid balances
These 'Other fees' may be covered by Federal Title IV funds (Financial Aid), if mandatory fees are fully paid and a remaining credit exists. However, federal regulations require MSOE to only apply Financial Aid to mandatory fees, unless authorized by the student.
A student can authorize to use Financial Aid to cover these 'Other Fees' by completing the
Credit Balance Authorization Form.

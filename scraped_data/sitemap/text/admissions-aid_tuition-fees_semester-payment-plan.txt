In order to enroll, you must be registered for the applicable term.
Log into your my.msoe account.
Select the 'Students' tab along the top of the screen.
From the left panel, choose 'Student Account Info'.
Click "Go to Transact".
Follow the on-screen instructions to complete enrollment.

Admitted Student Reception Parking
Admitted Student Reception Parking
MSOE's Admitted Student Receptions are brought to you by MSOE's Office of Student Affairs and Campus Inclusion. They are held in Dwight and Dian Diercks Computational Science Hall, 1025 N. Milwaukee Street, Milwaukee, WI.
Free, on-campus parking
You are welcome to park, for free, in any of MSOE's surface parking lots.
MSOE State Street A Parking Lot: 501 E. State St.
Grohmann Museum Visitor Lot:  NW corner of Milwaukee and State Streets. This lot is accessed from Milwaukee St.
MSOE Broadway Parking Lot: 1036 N. Broadway
Metered street parking
You can also find metered street parking near the Dwight and Dian Diercks Computational Science Hall.
Download "MKE Park," the City of Milwaukee mobile parking app.

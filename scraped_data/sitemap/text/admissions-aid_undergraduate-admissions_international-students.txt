International Students
Welcome! We’re so excited you’re interested in joining MSOE’s friendly and diverse community.
We define international students as non-residents who are here in the United States on a student, scholar or exchange visitor visa (F/J) or as a dependent on another non-resident visa. If that sounds like you, check out our admission requirements and other information for international students.
All residents should apply as a traditional student (either incoming freshman, transfer or graduate student).

Admitted Student Days
Admitted Student Days are your family’s all-access pass to life at MSOE. Students will step into their future as they join classes, connect with current students and faculty, and get an inside look at the projects, labs, and experiences that define an MSOE education.
Prospective students will spend the mornings with their chosen programs, engaging with current MSOE students and faculty. This time allows students to meet MSOE faculty, discuss their first-year curriculum, and learn more about student projects and internship/career opportunities.
Students and parents are also given an opportunity to meet with staff from
Financial Aid
,
Campus Life
,
Athletics
,
Residence Life
, and their Admissions Counselor, tour downtown Milwaukee, and make lasting connections with fellow incoming students.

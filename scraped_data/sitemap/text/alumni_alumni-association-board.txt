Alumni Association Board
The MSOE Alumni Association Board consists of alumni of various backgrounds that serve as the representatives of the MSOE alumni population. The board consists of no more than 21 members who serve in an advisory capacity.

Expectations of Alumni Association Board Members:
Attend board meetings
Participate as a member of a board committee
Be knowledgeable about and promote MSOE interests and programs by actively participating in MSOE events
Join and follow MSOE in official MSOE social media groups and remain active on platforms
Contribute to MSOE’s fundraising efforts
Student Alumni Association Board
Founded in the 2024-2025 academic year, the Student Alumni Association Board was built to promote MSOE pride and traditions through events and programs that benefit students, campus and the community.

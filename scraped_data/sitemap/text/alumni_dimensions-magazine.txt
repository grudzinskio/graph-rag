Dimensions Magazine
Dimensions magazine is MSOE’s primary campus news and feature publication. It is a compendium of students’ efforts and success, campus happenings and alumni updates.
As the flagship publication of Milwaukee School of Engineering,
Dimensions
features stories inspired by our mission and vision. It shines a light on our students, faculty, alumni and campus events, all while highlighting the values that unite us. The magazine is published twice annually and is mailed to all students at their permanent addresses, alumni, faculty and staff as well as other friends of MSOE. Additionally,
Dimensions
is located around campus on magazine racks.

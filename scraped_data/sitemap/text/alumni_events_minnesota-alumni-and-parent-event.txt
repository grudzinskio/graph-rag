Minnesota Alumni and Parent Event
Join us for a special regional event tailored for MSOE parents, families and alumni in the Minnesota and northwestern Wisconsin regions!
Come One, Come All!
Alumnus Neal Wunderlich '73, '74, founder and president of
Wunderlich-Malec Engineering
, generously welcomes MSOE parents, families and alumni in Minnesota and northwestern Wisconsin to the Minnesota Alumni and Parent Event! Join us for an evening of camaraderie, appetizers and drinks as we reconnect with fellow members of the MSOE community. Don't miss the opportunity to gain valuable insights from MSOE's president, Dr. Walz, about the future of our institution.
Date: Thursday, August 8, 2024
Location:
Wunderlich-Malec Engineering
Time:
5:30-7:30 p.m.

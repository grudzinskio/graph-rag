President's Golf Outing
Monday, June 8, 2026
Join Us for the 44th Annual President's Golf Outing!
The 44th Annual PGO Presidents' Golf Outing will take place on Monday, June 8, 2026, at the beautiful Oconomowoc Golf Club. This beloved tradition offers alumni and friends a fantastic opportunity to enjoy a memorable day in a relaxed and picturesque setting.
Last year, the outing successfully funded Presidents' Golf Outing scholarships, directly benefiting our exceptional students. We are deeply grateful for the unwavering support of our sponsors, enthusiastic golfers and dedicated staff who make this event a continued success.
In addition to a day of golf, we are excited to host our annual live auction, featuring a variety of exclusive and exciting items. All proceeds from the auction will go toward supporting the President's Golf Outing scholarships, helping to further the education of our outstanding students.
Thanks to your generosity, we can empower our students with the resources they need to excel. Don't miss this incredible day filled with camaraderie, competition, and community spirit—all in support of a great cause.

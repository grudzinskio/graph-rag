Athletics & Recreation Facilities
Health, wellness, fitness and recreation facilities for the MSOE community.
MSOE offers a variety of athletics and recreation facilities to enjoy during your time as a student, alumnus/a, faculty or staff member.
Facilities include:
Kern Center
(includes Hotchkin Arena, field house, ice arena, workout areas and running track)
Viets Field
MSOE Raiders Stadium
Raiders Field
Facility Hours of Operation:
Kern Center Academic Year Hours:
Monday–Thursday: 6:30 a.m.–Midnight
Friday: 6:30 a.m.–10 p.m.
Saturday: 8 a.m.–8 p.m.
Sunday: 10 a.m.–8 p.m.
Spring Break Week Hours
March 14–22 | Swipe access only for faculty, staff and students
Wednesday, March 18 | Open 3–9 p.m.
Viets Field Academic Year Hours:
Monday–Thursday: 6:30 a.m.–9 p.m.
Friday, Saturday & Sunday: 6:30 a.m.–8 p.m.
To check availability, please refer to the hours above as well as
25live
, MSOE’s official event and room reservation system. MSOE’s athletics schedule is available at
go-raiders.com
.
Viets Field hours are subject to change without notice due to weather conditions or campus/external events. Please check the 25live Viets Field calendar to see what times are not available due to team practices, events, sports camps, etc.

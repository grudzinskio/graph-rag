MSOE Athletics
Whether you're in the heat of the competition or cheering from the sidelines, MSOE offers year-round opportunities to get involved. With over 25 varsity programs competing in NCAA Division III, the whole community can come together and enjoy.
MSOE is home to the Raiders! Our teams compete in the following conferences at the NCAA Division III level:
Northern Athletics Collegiate Conference (NACC)
Northern Collegiate Hockey Association (NCHA)
College Conference of Illinois & Wisconsin (CCIW)

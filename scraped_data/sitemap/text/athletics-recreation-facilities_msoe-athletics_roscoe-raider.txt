Meet MSOE's Mascot!
Roscoe Raider, MSOE's mascot, has been at the helm since the mid-1990s. He can be seen at campus events, athletic events and navigating the seas of MSOE's campus. Roscoe is a goofy, swashbuckling pirate with a deep love of the university.
Give Roscoe a high-five, pose for a photo or selfie and show your Raider pride when you see him out and about!

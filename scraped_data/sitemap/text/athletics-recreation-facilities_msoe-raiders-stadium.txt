MSOE Raiders Stadium
MSOE Raiders Stadium opened at Maslowski Park in 2019 in Glendale, Wisconsin and features a synthetic turf baseball field, dugouts, lights, a press box and wrap-around seating from the first to third-base sides of the field for more than 300 spectators. The field is home to MSOE’s men’s baseball team as well as Nicolet High School’s baseball team.  MSOE Raiders Stadium was funded primarily by a gift from Dr. Kathleen Ruehlow ’72. Dr. Alan Ruud ’69, Catalyst Construction, and Gene Sheedy also supported the project. Raiders Stadium is located at Richard E. Maslowski Glendale Community Park,  2200 W Bender Rd., Glendale, WI. Raiders Stadium is wheelchair accessible.

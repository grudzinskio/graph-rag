Raiders Field
Raiders Field opened in 2023, and is home to MSOE’s women’s softball team. It replaces the university’s existing softball field, which is in the heart of campus in downtown Milwaukee. It features a natural grass and dirt field, two dugouts, a single lane batting cage and bullpen on each side of the field, press box, ticket window, concessions and seating for 250 spectators. Also at the site are Frisbee golf holes, walking paths and green space. Raiders Field was funded entirely through the philanthropy of Dr. Kathleen Ruehlow ’72, MSOE Regent; MSOE Regent Matthew ’99 and Jodi ’98 Burow; and Fiduciary Management Inc. Raiders Field is located at 1010 N. Milwaukee St., Milwaukee, WI.  Raiders Field is wheelchair accessible.

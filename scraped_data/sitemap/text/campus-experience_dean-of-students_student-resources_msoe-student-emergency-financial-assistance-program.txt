MSOE Student Emergency Financial Assistance Program
MSOE understands that students may face financial distress due to extenuating or emergency circumstances. When financial distress impacts a student’s ability to meet immediate and essential expenses or jeopardizes their ability to continue enrollment, MSOE seeks to support students to the best of our abilities.
In some cases MSOE is able to provide short-term, one-time financial support through generous contributions from our donors. Funding is limited and may not eliminate the student’s entire financial distress. While we are committed to helping any MSOE student in distress, financial assistance may not be available for every request.
What can be covered
Unexpected Personal Emergencies
Examples of covered expenses:
Emergency housing or utilities
Food insecurity
Medical or mental health emergencies
Transportation/car repair required to attend class/work
Technology replacement (laptop) when required for coursework
Temporary childcare due to crisis
Generally not covered:
Non-essential bills, credit card debt, entertainment
Ongoing monthly expenses
Small Institutional Balance Gaps
Examples of covered expenses:
Modest unpaid tuition/fee balances preventing registration
Loss of aid mid-term or merit aid due to temporary circumstances
Final-term balance preventing graduation
Generally not covered:
Ongoing tuition and standard fees
Eligibility
Currently enrolled degree-seeking MSOE student
Demonstrated unexpected financial hardship
Situation presents a credible risk to enrollment or academic progress
In good academic standing or on a documented success plan
Has pursued or exhausted other available resources (FAFSA, payment plan, short-term loans, family support, etc.)
Generally limited to one award per academic year (exceptions by committee)
Students must have accepted their full financial aid package (including all federal loans)
Application for Emergency Assistance
A member of the committee will respond in 1-2 business days to discuss your request. In most cases, a final decision is made within 10 business days.

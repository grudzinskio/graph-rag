Emergency Assistance Request Form
MSOE understands that students may face financial distress due to extenuating or emergency circumstances. When financial distress impacts a student’s abilities to meet immediate and essential expenses or jeopardizes their ability to continue enrollment, MSOE seeks to support while helping students navigate circumstances.
This form is not intended for persistent financial strain, but rather isolated circumstances due to extenuating or emergency situations. Examples include but are not limited to; housing, utilities, food, medical expenses, transportation, required materials for courses, etc. Requests for ongoing tuition and fees, credit card debt, entertainment or non-essential utilities will not be covered.
Please note that funding is limited and we may be unable to honor every request. Funding that is awarded may not eliminate the student’s entire financial distress.

Senior Celebration
Celebrate all year with MSOE's senior class!
Your senior year should be a time of celebration and transition. And whatever comes next - a job, graduate program, or new opportunity - you'll carry MSOE memories with you. MSOE's Senior Celebration spans all year with special events and resources to commemorate your best year yet!

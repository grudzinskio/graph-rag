Welcome to... Welcome Week!
You're officially on campus, we're glad to have you here! Welcome Week is jam-packed with fun events and unforgettable experiences designed to ease you into life as a Raider. From making new friends, establishing relationships with key faculty and staff or getting your groove on, this week sets the foundation for success for your next four years.
Grab your sunglasses, your smile, and your adventurous spirit ... it's time to get this Welcome Week started! Check back for the full schedule events this summer!

Grohmann Tower
Grohmann Tower is MSOE’s apartment-style housing option.
233 E. Juneau Ave.
Grohmann Tower is MSOE’s apartment-style housing.
It comprises 11 residential floors and features studios and 1- and 2-bedroom apartments.
All apartments include a full kitchen and bathroom.
All apartments come furnished (e.g., bed, dresser, desk, chair). Utilities (i.e., heat, air conditioning, electricity, water) are included and resident-controlled.
Semester, Academic Year, and Summer contracts are available.
Floors 4-10 have access to a community laundry room, while floors 11-14 include an in-unit washer and dryer.
Each floor (GT 4-10) has at least one Resident Assistant who leads and plans activities, builds community on the floor, and supports a safe and welcoming living environment.
Apartments include
native wireless routers
; personal routers are prohibited. Students also have access to
Wi-Fi across campus.
Residents are responsible only for their share of the rent, regardless of how many are assigned.
Multiple groups can live in Grohmann Tower, floors 11-14.
Third-year students and beyond (e.g., juniors, seniors, graduate students).
Faculty and staff may request to live in Grohmann Tower if space is available.
Roommates and Occupancy
Studio and 1-bedroom apartments on floors 4 through 10 have a maximum of 3 occupants.
2-bedroom apartments on floors 6, 8, and 10 have a maximum of 4 occupants.
1-bedroom apartments on floors 11 through 14 have a maximum of 3 occupants.
2-bedroom apartments on floors 11 through 14 have a maximum of 5 occupants.

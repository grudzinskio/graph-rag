Diverse Dining Experiences
The third floor of the MSOE Campus Center features the Robert Spitzer Dining Commons. This is the primary dining area for resident and commuter students, faculty, staff, and campus visitors. When entering the servery, students will swipe their MSOE ID card at the entrance. Guests can purchase a meal pass at the register.
MSOE challenges students to maximize their potential. This challenge requires healthy and nutritious dining options and collaborative spaces. Sustainable practices and locally sourced ingredients meet students' expectations for healthy eating options. The Robert Spitzer Dining Commons is an intentionally designed space to increase and enhance food service capacity to support students, faculty, and staff. The Dining Commons fosters a community with a variety of tasty dining options, places to meet, study spaces, and a game room for socializing. The Robert Spitzer Dining Commons is the university's central gathering location.
Visit the MSOE Dining Services website to see meal plan options as well as nutrition information.

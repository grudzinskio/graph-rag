What We Provide
For students living in on-campus residence halls, the university provides a dresser, desk and chair, bedframe and
twin XL
mattress, and internet connection. All residential buildings include
native wireless routers
; personal routers are prohibited. Students also have access to
Wi-Fi across campus
.

Juneau Village Towers housing here
.
Customizing Your Room
Some residents customize their living space with a loft bed (for MLH only), futon or other MSOE-approved items in their living environment. Make it your own; make it feel like home.

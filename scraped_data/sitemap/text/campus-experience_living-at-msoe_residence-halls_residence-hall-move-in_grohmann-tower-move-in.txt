Grohmann Tower Move-In
Fall 2018 move-in details for the Grohmann Tower Honors Program.
Welcome! We are very excited to welcome you to the Grohmann Tower of MSOE. We want to make move-in day as smooth as possible for everyone. Simply follow the steps listed below and moving in can be as easy as 1-2-3!
Step 1:
Know Your Apartment Number and Move-In Time.
Make sure you know which apartment you are in. If you are unsure, please give us a call at (414) 277-7228.
Step 2: Check In!
Check in at the first floor lounge of the
Grohmann Tower
, 233 E. Juneau Ave. Here you will pick up your room key and move-in packet. You may
park in front of the building
or in the parking lot/alley on the east side of the Tower. You may also park in the 1214 Water St. lot, please get a parking pass from the Grohmann Tower front desk to park there.
Step 3: Unload!
Carts will be available for check-out at the Grohmann Tower desk to assist with moving personal belongings. After you’re finished, return the cart to the front desk and move your vehicle to the
1214 Water St. lot
to keep the unloading area clear.
Enjoy the rest of your summer and we look forward to seeing you on campus soon!
We appreciate your patience and cooperation during move-in. If you have any questions about move-in, housing or meal plans, please call the Grohmann Tower Office at (414) 277-7228 or email
gagliano@msoe.edu
.

Campus Safety
MSOE Public Safety Dept.
The area around the MSOE campus is one of the safest areas found in Milwaukee, however, crime can occur anywhere.
For the security of the MSOE community, the MSOE Public Safety Department is on duty 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Officers patrol campus on foot, bicycle and by car.

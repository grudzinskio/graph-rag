Campus Shuttle, SHIP and Mass Transit
Transportation designed to help you get around the MSOE campus safe and efficiently.
MSOE operates a shuttle service for all members of the campus community to provide safe transportation to and from all campus buildings and parking lots. The
Campus Shuttle
provides transportation during the week (Sunday through Thursday), while
SHIP
(Safely Hauling Individuals Places) provides expanded coverage on Friday and Saturday.
Note: Requests for transportation services are processed in the order they are received. Individuals requesting service occasionally may experience a delay due to the number of requests pending, the amount of traffic on the road and the weather conditions. Unexpected staffing shortages or mechanical breakdown make impact service availability. Transportation is not offered during student breaks, quarter breaks, holiday breaks and over the summer months.
Campus Shuttle
The Campus Shuttle provides service to and from MSOE campus buildings, parking lots, and the Viets Parking Complex. Service boundaries are: N. Water St. on the west, N. Marshall St. on the east, E. Ogden St. on the north, and E. Wisconsin Ave. on the south (
view map
). The Campus Shuttle may go outside the designated service area to the following locations, if on-campus conveyance volume and weather conditions permit:
Milwaukee Intermodal Station, 433 W. St. Paul Ave.
Documented medical appointments or emergency treatment at:
Aurora Sinai Medical Center, 945 N. 12th St.
Aurora Health Center, 1575 N. River Center Dr.
Requests for Service
All requests must originate or terminate at an MSOE building, parking lot, or the student parking structure.
To request Campus Shuttle service call (414) 277-7464.
Service Hours:
Sunday through Thursday: 2 p.m. - 10:30 p.m.
Friday: 9:00 a.m. - 5 p.m.
SHIP
SHIP is MSOE’s expanded weekend transportation service. A valid MSOE ID is required and must be shown to the SHIP operator before the transport takes place. The boundaries for SHIP are North Prospect Avenue to the east, North 12th Street (Marquette University) to the west, East Brady St. to the north, and West St. Paul Ave. to the south.
SHIP Service Map
To request SHIP service: call (414) 277-SHIP.
Service Hours:
Saturday: 5 p.m. until 2 a.m.
Note: SHIP service is suspended between 9 p.m. and 9:30 p.m.
Area Mass Transit
Area mass transit includes the bus and trolley lines, operated by the
Milwaukee County Transportation System
. Many route stops are within or near campus.
For passenger rail service information, see the
Amtrak website
.

Code Blue Emergency Phones
MSOE Public Safety Dept.
The Code Blue Emergency telephones offer direct contact with Public Safety. Once the red emergency button is pressed, the telephone connection to Public Safety is instantaneous, and there is no need to dial a telephone number.  These telephones can be used to report a criminal incident, fire, illness, suspicious person, or any other type of emergency on our campus.

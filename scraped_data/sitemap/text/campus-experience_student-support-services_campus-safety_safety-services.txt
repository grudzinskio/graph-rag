Safety Services
Since safety is a top priority when away from home, MSOE offers safety services for students, faculty and staff with Rave Guardian and Rave Emergency Alerts.
You can invite friends and family to “virtually” walk with you, text information to Public Safety, access emergency numbers and receive important alerts. Details on how to access these safety services are below.
Information on the safety app, how to download and FAQs.
Mobile alerts designed to keep you updated on severe weather, issues or emergency alerts.
In the event of a long-duration emergency situation, non-critical updates will be posted on this page.

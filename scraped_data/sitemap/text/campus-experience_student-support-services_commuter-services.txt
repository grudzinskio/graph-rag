Commuter Services
As a commuter student, you have access to all of the same social and academic resources as students who live on campus. From places to study, ways to get involved, lockers to store your belongings, or services to help you be successful, there’s a place for you on campus.
Lounges and Study Spaces
Campus is full of lounges and study spaces - Campus Center, Library, and Tree Lounge just to name a few. Stop by to kick back and relax or study for your next exam.
Meal Plans
Learn more about our new commuter meal plans (Block Meal Plans)
Advising
As a commuter student, you face different challenges than students that live on-campus. Get help navigating campus, getting connected to resources, and finding ways to make the most out of your time on campus by visiting the
Wellness Center
and talking with a Student Support Advisor.
Involvement
Whether you’re interested in advancing mobility on land, in air, sea, and space, designing opportunities for health promotion and prevention in the community, constructing a steel bridge for a national competition, or showing off your gaming skills, there is a way to fuel or find your passion. Check out our
student orgs
or stop by the
Student Life Office
to explore areas of involvement.
Parking
Save time getting to class, avoid parking tickets, and remember where you parked by securing a campus parking permit.
Lockers
Give your back a break. Store your belongings in one of several locker locations on campus. You bring the lock, we provide the locker.

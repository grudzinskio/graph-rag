Recent Projects
IT is committed to developing new solutions for the campus community
IT is constantly engaging in a variety of projects designed to improve the technology experience on campus. Below are a few of our most recent projects and initiatives.
In early 2017, IT designed, built and installed several charging stations around campus.
Designed to charge any potential device, these stations are equipped with micro USB, reversible USB, Apple standard and Lighting chargers.
Charging stations are located in Campus Center and there are plans for expansion.
Over the summer break, IT worked to replace the A/V system in the Kern Center.
Over the summer of 2017, IT upgraded our Jenzabar EX system and JICS portal - MyMSOE. The upgrade included making the MyMSOE portal responsive to mobile devices, allowing users to access the information from any device.

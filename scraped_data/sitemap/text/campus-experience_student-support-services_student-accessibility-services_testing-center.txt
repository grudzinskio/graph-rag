Information for students requiring testing accommodations
The primary responsibility of the Testing Center is to assist MSOE with meeting legally required needs of students with disabilities. Students with a documented disability, chronic medical condition, or mental health concern may request testing accommodations through Student Accessibility Services (SAS). The Coordinator of SAS, Angela Moureau, can be reached at 414-277-7281 or
moureau@msoe.edu
. You must have prior approval from SAS to use testing accommodations.
Location and Hours of Operation
The Testing Center is located on the second floor of the Kern Center, 1245 North Broadway, Suite K-250.
The Testing Center is open only when testing appointments are scheduled. Standard operating hours are Monday through Friday, 8:00 a.m.-4:30 p.m.
Services
Testing Accommodations can include, but are not limited to:
Extended test time
Non-classroom testing environment
Alternate test format
Use of text-to-speech
Reduced distraction aids
Schedule requests for any other students unable to take a classroom test at the scheduled date/time will be granted as space and staffing allows.

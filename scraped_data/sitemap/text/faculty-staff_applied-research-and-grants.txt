Office of Applied Research and Grants
The Office of Applied Research and Grants at MSOE serves as a hub for advancing innovation, applied learning, and research collaboration. We assist faculty and staff throughout the life cycle of external funding, with services provided through Pre-Award and Post-Award functions.
What We Do
We support faculty and staff at every stage of the research lifecycle, including:
Identifying funding opportunities
Reviewing eligibility and application requirements
Developing proposals, including writing, editing, formatting
Developing and reviewing proposal budgets
Facilitating institutional approvals
Assuring compliance
Submitting proposals
Negotiating awards and contracts
Managing post-award procedures and reporting

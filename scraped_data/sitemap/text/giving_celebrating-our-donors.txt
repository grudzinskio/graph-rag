Celebrating our Donors
MSOE is grateful for all of our generous donors who have expressed their confidence in our university by making a gift.
Recognition Societies
Recognition societies exist to honor those who help keep MSOE strong and growing through philanthropy. Members of our recognition societies partner with the university to build the foundation for student success. Their investments enable MSOE to grow and flourish.
President’s Circle
Established in 2020, the President’s Circle is comprised of alumni and friends whose annual support ensures students have an extraordinary experience at MSOE. Our members recognize the significance of an MSOE education and our vision to prepare the next generation of leaders to solve the diverse challenges of the 21st century.
The President’s Circle
recognizes cumulative annual leadership gifts of $1,000
or more to any purpose at MSOE from July 1 to June 30 of each year. By becoming a member of the President’s Circle, you are taking a leadership role in supporting MSOE’s mission and for that we are incredibly grateful.
Levels:
Bronze: $1,000 - $2,499
Silver: $2,500 - $4,999
Gold: $5,000 - $9,999
Platinum: $10,000 +
1903 Society
The 1903 Society recognizes the thoughtfulness and generosity of alumni and friends with estate gifts benefiting MSOE. Estate gifts may include bequests, life insurance, retirement-plan benefits, and gifts of real estate among other opportunities. Members of the 1903 Society help to ensure opportunities are available for the next generation of Raiders.
Parent Leadership Circle
Established in 2024, the
Parent Leadership Circle
(PLC) was designed to engage our community of parents and families of current students. Parents involved with the PLC recognize the importance of philanthropy and involvement on campus. Our PLC strives to create opportunities and events for parents to connect and support one another while contributing to the excellence of all students’ experiences at MSOE. The Parent Leadership Circle recognizes current parents who have donated $1,000 or more to any purpose at MSOE from July 1 to June 30 of each year. By becoming a part of the PLC, you are contributing to the success of all students.

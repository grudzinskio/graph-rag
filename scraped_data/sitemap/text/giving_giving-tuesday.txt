Let's Celebrate Giving Tuesday 2025!
Join us on
December 2nd
, when the MSOE community comes together to celebrate Giving Tuesday, a global celebration of giving. Each year, Giving Tuesday is a time for alumni, parents, students, faculty, staff and friends of the university to make a difference at MSOE through a community service project, showing gratitude to those in your community, giving to the area on campus you are most passionate about and/or spreading the word. This year, the MSOE Fraternity & Sorority Council (FSC) and the Student Alumni Association Board (SAAB) are partnering with NourishMKE to hold a food drive, collecting non-perishable food items for the Milwaukee community.
How to Get Involved:
Make a Gift:
Every contribution, no matter the amount, makes a significant difference to our campus.
Show Appreciation:
Reach out with a thank-you email, text, phone call or card to someone in the MSOE community.
Donate Food:
Drop off non-perishable food items outside of the Raider Shop or in the lobby of each on-campus residence hall for NourishMKE from Nov. 3-Dec. 2.
Thank you for supporting 2024 Giving Tuesday!

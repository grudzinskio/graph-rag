MSOE Giving Day
Thank you for your support on MSOE Giving Day!
Get Ready for Giving Day on April 16, 2026!
Each year, MSOE challenges our alumni, faculty, staff, students, families and friends to come together during our annual Giving Day to support the university and the students we serve. In 2025, the generosity of
2,524 donors resulted in contributions exceeding $900,000, making it MSOE's most impactful Giving Day to date
.
For 24 hours, we rally support for numerous areas of our campus, including academic departments, annual scholarships, athletics, student clubs and organizations, the campus experience, the Parent and Family Fund and
STEM programming
. Thanks to challenges and gift matches, donations have a greater impact during these 24 hours, making this day very special!
Support Giving Day!
Consider supporting Giving Day by contributing a matching gift towards an area of your passion on campus!

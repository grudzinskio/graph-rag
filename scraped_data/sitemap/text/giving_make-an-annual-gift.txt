Make an Annual Gift
Annual Gifts make an incredible impact on the educational experience MSOE provides to its students. You can designate your gift to the Fund for MSOE (link), to scholarships, or to the department, program, organization, team or cause of your choice. There are a number of ways to make your annual gift.
Make and Online Gift
- One of the easiest ways to make your gift to MSOE is making a secure gift via a credit or debit card via our secure giving website.  You can make a one-time gift or set up a recurring annual or monthly gift to the designation of your choice.
Mail – If you prefer to make your gift via check, please make your check payable to “Milwaukee School of Engineering” and specify your gift designation in the memo line or in an accompanying letter.  Checks may be mailed to:
Office of University Advancement
1025 North Broadway
Milwaukee, WI 53202
Appreciated Stock and Securities – Many donors find it advantageous to fund their annual gift by gifting appreciated securities directly to MSOE.  If you would like to donate stock please contact MSOE Development at
development@msoe.edu
or 414-277-7148.
IRA Required Minimum Distribution – If you are required to take a distribution from your IRA and you would prefer not to take the income, you can direct your RMD directly to MSOE to fund your gift.  To learn more, contact MSOE Development at
development@msoe.edu
or 414-277-7148.

Departments & Programs
Many donors choose to direct their giving to a specific academic department, program or student organization at MSOE. You can designate your gift for the general use of the department or program, or support a restricted fund which may provide resources for senior design projects, classroom or lab equipment, or faculty and student development opportunities.
You can make a gift to support a particular department or program on
MSOE’s online giving page
by selecting “other” when asked what you would like to support, and write in the department, program or organization of your choice.
Examples of departments and programs you could support include:
Athletics
Civil and Architectural Engineering and Construction Management
Diversity, Equity, Inclusion and Belonging
Electrical Engineering and Computer Science
Grohmann Museum
Humanities, Social Science and Communication

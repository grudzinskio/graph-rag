Parent and Family Fund
Welcome to the Raider Family
When your family chose MSOE, you did not just commit to an excellent education for your child, you joined a community of passionate, diverse, and connected parents. Parents are an integral part of the MSOE community and help make the MSOE experience an extraordinary one.
We encourage all our parents and families to get involved with campus to enrich your experience as a member of the MSOE community.
The Parent and Family Fund supports six different areas on campus that impact the student experience. All gifts made to the Parent and Family Fund go directly back to campus and will impact your student while they are at MSOE.

Senior Class Gift Campaign
The Senior Class Gift Campaign is designed to inspire graduating seniors to leave their legacy at MSOE by making a gift that will have a lasting impact on future Raiders. Gifts of any size can make a difference, but those who donate $25 or more at any point in their senior year will receive a red and black cord to wear at Commencement.
On behalf of the entire MSOE community, we'd like to thank the spring graduating class of 2024 for participating in the Senior Class Gift Campaign! A total of 83 graduates donated toward the campaign, doubling our participation from the previous year!

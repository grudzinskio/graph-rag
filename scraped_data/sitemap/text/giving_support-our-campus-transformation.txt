Support our Campus Transformation
The past two decades have seen an incredible transformation of MSOE’s campus in support of our educational mission. Just since 2017, we have invested more than $85 million in campus projects to create a new building dedicated to artificial intelligence and next-generation technologies, transform our oldest residence hall,  and construct a state-of-the-art STEM outreach center for K-12 education.
The majority of these projects were made possible thanks to generous private gifts from MSOE alumni, friends, faculty and staff, corporate partners and charitable foundations. MSOE relies on philanthropy to provide outstanding facilities that support the work of our students and faculty.
If you would like to learn more about how to support MSOE’s continued campus transformation as well as how to make a naming gift in one of our facilities, please contact MSOE Development at
development@msoe.edu
or (414) 277-7148.

The Fund for MSOE
The Fund for MSOE makes an incredible positive impact on the university and in the success of the students we serve.
Gifts to the Fund for MSOE are unrestricted which allows the university to direct funding to areas of the greatest need. From student scholarships, program support, assisting our world class faculty and more, your tax-deductible gift to the Fund for MSOE ensures the university has the tools it needs to rethink what’s possible.
You may make a one-time gift, set up a recurring gift or make a multi-year pledge to the Fund for MSOE.
To make your one-time gift or to set up a recurring monthly gift, visit our secure online
giving page
.

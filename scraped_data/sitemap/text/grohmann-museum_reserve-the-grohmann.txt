Grohmann Museum Request Form
The premier event setting on the MSOE Campus, The Grohmann Museum can accommodate groups up to 60 for sit-down dinners, 80 for lectures/presentations, and 120 for ‘roaming’ events with cocktails and passed appetizers.  The Museum is available for tours as well as corporate and non-profit events (no private parties or weddings, please). Any filming or photography must be approved by the Museum. Please complete the form below to request approval and rates. Our Visitor Services Manager will contact you shortly with more information.

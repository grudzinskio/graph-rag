Group Tours Request Form
The Grohmann Museum welcomes group tours and field trips during our weekday business hours. Docents are subject to availability, please allow four weeks advanced notice for guided tours. Docent-led tours last 60-90 minutes and are included with regular admission rates for minimum of 12 adults ($5/each) and seniors ($3/each).  Please note that discounted or free admission vouchers are not valid for docent-led tours and regular admission rates apply. Evening and weekend tours are subject to an additional staffing fee.
School field trips cost $3/student and chaperones are granted one free ticket per every ten children.
Art educators at the Grohmann Museum are happy to arrange hands-on art activities for schools and Scout groups as part of their Museum visit (space is limited to 25 students). Please let us know if your group is working on a badge requirement. We are knowledgeable on Boy Scout and Girl Scout badges that incorporate visual arts and S.T.E.A.M.
K-12 Groups may also schedule a combined visit with the MSOE STEM Center. Students will spend half of their visit at the Grohmann Museum and the other half doing a hands-on activity at the STEM Center. Please note in your form if you are interested in visiting both locations and we will confirm availability with the STEM Center.

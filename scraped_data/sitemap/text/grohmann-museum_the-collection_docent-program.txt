Docent Program
Join us in telling the story of labor throughout history by becoming a docent.
The Grohmann Museum has a team of volunteer docents who support our mission by guiding tours, educating visitors, and helping engage the public with our exhibitions. Our docents play a pivotal roles at the Museum by providing tours for various groups including K-12 students, senior centers, and travel groups.
This role is a great fit for those who have an interest in art, history, engineering, or education. Prior docent experience is not necessary and training is provided. Docents meet monthly to discuss upcoming tour shifts and participate in educational sessions at the Museum.
To learn more and apply to become a docent, please email Marina Bernovich, Curator of Education, at
bernovich@msoe.edu

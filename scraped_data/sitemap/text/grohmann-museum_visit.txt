Location
The Grohmann Museum is located on the NE corner of Broadway and State St. on the MSOE campus in Milwaukee.
Parking
Free parking for Grohmann Museum patrons is available east of the museum at the NW corner of Milwaukee and State Streets. This lot is accessed from Milwaukee St.  Please furnish vehicle information, including license plate number, at the front desk when using this lot.
Additional street parking can be found nearby. Please refer to the
city of Milwaukee's parking services
for the governance of these spaces.
Accessibility
The building is fully wheelchair accessible. Two wheelchairs and 25 folding gallery stools are available to borrow at the front desk.

Purchase tickets at the admissions desk or

Group Tours
The Grohmann Museum welcomes group tours and field trips during our weekday business hours. Docent-led tours typically last between 60-90 minutes and are included with regular admission rates for
minimum of 12 adults and or/seniors
. Field trips cost $3 per student and chaperones are granted one free ticket per every ten children. Docents are subject to availability, please allow four to six weeks advanced notice for guided tours. Learn more and
schedule your group tour here
.

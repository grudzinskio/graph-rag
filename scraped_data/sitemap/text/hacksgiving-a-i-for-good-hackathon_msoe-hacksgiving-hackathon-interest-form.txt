MSOE Hacksgiving Community Partner Interest Form
The MSOE Mindset identifies graduates as leaders of character; responsible professionals; passionate learners; and value creators. In addition, our vision challenges use to set the standard for preparing leaders to solve the diverse technical challenges of the 21st century. The MSOE Hacksgiving Hackathon provides a forum for community partners to bring forward problems, challenges and opportunities that they may not have the resources or technical expertise to solve. We are excited to unleash the fearless problem-solving abilities of our students to meet those challenges, enrich our community and learn during the process using their creativity and application-oriented talents and skills, with a special emphasis on technology and artificial intelligence.
To learn more about examples of the types of A.I. problems we and our students are capable of solving, please visit the
Rosie Supercomputer Super Challenge webpage
and the
MSOE AI Club research project page

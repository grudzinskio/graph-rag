Information for Delegates
Milwaukee School of Engineering is pleased to welcome delegates from other colleges and universities who will help honor and celebrate the inauguration of Eric T. Baumgartner, Ph.D., as the sixth president of MSOE.
The inauguration ceremony will take place on Friday, April 24, 2026 at 2 p.m. at the Kern Center, 1245 N. Broadway, Milwaukee, WI 53202.
Formal invitations to presidents of other campuses were emailed in early February. Understanding that college and university presidents hold very busy schedules, institutions may designate an alternate to represent their campus as an official delegate.
Delegates are asked to provide their own academic regalia. Information on enrobing and other details for the day will be sent with your confirmation. All are invited to the reception immediately following the inauguration ceremony. Delegates should register online using the link shared in the invitation. Please contact
president@msoe.edu
if you have questions or require assistance.
Inauguration Celebration RSVP
Due to space limitations, registration is required for the Inauguration Celebration. Kindly refer to the RSVP details included in your inauguration invitation.

Career Connections Center
We want to help you find a career path that will lead to a successful, happy future.
The Career Connections Center works hard to make your internship and full-time job search as easy as possible. Not only do we bring employers right on campus to recruit you, we can also help you be their candidate of choice.
Find valuable guides and examples to help you with your internship and full-time job search, resume, cover letter, Handshake and LinkedIn, interview skills, career fair prep and so much more.
As an alumni, assistance from the Career Connections Center is always available. Whether you’re still looking for your first-full-time job after graduation, looking for a new position, company, or industry, or making a complete career change, we’re happy to help.
Thank you for your interest in MSOE students and alumni – we invite you to recruit at MSOE for your internship and full-time job opportunities.
Learn about how the Career Connections Center helps students and alumni gain valuable employment opportunities while at MSOE, a rewarding career upon graduation, and receive a significant ROI for their hard work earning their degree.
The Career Connections Center is one of the MSOE advantages. At MSOE, students and alumni get more out of their degree. More personal attention. More practical experience. More career power. And the advantage is measurable: Just consider our enviable 96% graduate outcomes rate and $77,161 average starting salary (ranked #17 "Best U.S. College for High Salaries" by the Wall Street Journal).

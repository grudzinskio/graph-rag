Alumni Network
We offer services to alumni for life!
If you are an alumni who is seeking assistance with next steps or a career change, MSOE Career Services is here to help you.  Upon graduating MSOE, you receive our services for life! Please contact our office if you would like to schedule an appointment (in-person, phone, or video conferencing). We can offer you assistance in changing careers, application materials, search strategies, and much more. You are more than welcome to utilize our
online training materials
, and create a free account on our online platform
Handshake.
Welcome home! Even though you’re no longer on campus every day, you’re an important part of MSOE. You extend our reach into the wider world. We want to know where you are and what you’re doing, plus, we have great news to share with you.
Stay connected with MSOE and fellow Alumni. Pay it forward to future MSOE alums by giving back to MSOE in talent or resources. Learn about others who have done so, donate to the MSOE Annual Fund or learn more about other ways to give.

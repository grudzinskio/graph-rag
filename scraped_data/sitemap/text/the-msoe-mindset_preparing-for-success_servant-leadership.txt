Servant-Leadership
Helping to foster the growth of civic-minded professionals!
In 2004, MSOE established the Pieper Family Endowed Chair for Servant-Leadership, thanks to a major gift from the Suzanne and Richard Pieper Family Foundation. Assistant Professor Gene Wright currently holds the Pieper Family Endowed Chair for Servant-Leadership. He is responsible for helping integrate this leadership philosophy throughout the social fabric and culture of the university.
The Pieper Family Foundation endowment helps fund projects that enable our students to integrate what they learn in the classroom while practicing the
Principles of Servant-Leadership
to serve the
Milwaukee community and beyond
. Servant-leaders have a fundamental commitment to serving others with integrity and humility while encouraging excellence and growth in those whom they lead.

How to Share Your Opportunity
MSOE encourages posting volunteer opportunities on our web platform, Handshake. Registering within the Handshake system is cost free and a fast and easy process – start here:
Getting started with Handshake.
Some great Handshake guides:
Create My User Account
Register My Company
Connect with Schools
Post a Job
Manage Job Postings
A great benefit of posting in the Handshake system is that it’s used by almost every college and university in Wisconsin, along with hundreds more nationwide, so be sure to connect with and post your opportunities with a number of schools to increase your candidate pool.
Additional Tips for Volunteer Opportunities
Use an email with a company/organization domain (avoid Gmail, yahoo, etc. where possible)
Reach out to
Julie Way
to let her know when you’ve registered, so she can immediately connect you with MSOE in the Handshake system.
Be sure to select
VOLUNTEER
when choosing the job type. Handshake Help Center
Please note that submitting a volunteer request does not guarantee volunteers from MSOE.
